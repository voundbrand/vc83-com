# Headline and Body Formulas

## Headline Formulas

1. Specific Benefit + Specific Audience
   - "How [audience] gets [outcome] in [time] without [pain]"
2. Proof-Led Observation
   - "[Data point] is costing [audience] [loss] - here's the fix"
3. Reason-Why Promise
   - "Why [common attempt] fails - and what works instead"
4. Offer + Mechanism
   - "Get [outcome] with [mechanism], not [inferior alternative]"

## Lead Paragraph Patterns

1. Problem-first with evidence
   - Start with one factual pain signal.
2. Outcome-first with mechanism
   - Name desired result, then how it's achieved.
3. Contrast lead
   - Current state vs improved state in one paragraph.

## Body Block Pattern

For each block:

1. Claim
2. Reason why
3. Evidence
4. Transition to next block

## CTA Pattern

- Action verb + specific outcome + low-friction next step.
- Example shape: "Book a 20-minute strategy call and leave with a quantified copy gap report."
