## SCRIPT

### 1. Name the External Barrier (30-45 sec)

> "Here's what you think is stopping you from scaling:
>
> 'There are too many agencies. Clients have been burned before -- they don't trust us. And the bigger agencies have more people, more budget, more resources. I can't compete.'
>
> It feels real. You see new agencies popping up every week. You hear clients say 'my last agency did nothing.' You look at your solo operation and think there's no way you can keep up.
>
> It feels like the world is working against you."

### 2. What They've Tried (30-45 sec)

> "So you've tried competing on price -- cutting your rates to win clients. You've tried doing everything manually to 'control quality.' You've tried adding more services to seem bigger than you are.
>
> None of it worked because:
>
> Competing on price killed your margins. Manual delivery capped you at 15 clients. And adding more services just meant more work for you -- not more revenue."

### 3. Why It Doesn't Work (30-45 sec)

> "Here's what nobody tells you:
>
> The agencies that struggle aren't struggling because the market is saturated. They're struggling because they sell services. Hours for money. Custom builds every time.
>
> The agencies that scale? They sell products. A white-labeled platform with pre-configured AI agents. Same setup, new brand. Deploy in minutes.
>
> The market isn't saturated. The service model is. The product model has barely started.
>
> And here's the thing about competing with bigger agencies: they're bigger because they have more people doing manual work. You don't need more people. You need a system that replaces the manual work entirely. One person with the right platform beats a team of ten with the wrong one."

### 4. The Truth (45-60 sec)

> "Let me give you the numbers:
>
> 60 to 80 percent of inbound calls to small businesses go unanswered. A plumber misses a call -- that's EUR 2,000 in lost revenue. A dentist -- EUR 500 per missed appointment. A salon -- EUR 150 per no-show.
>
> Your clients aren't asking 'do I need AI?' They're bleeding money every single day because nobody answers the phone after 5pm. Nobody follows up on the quote they sent. Nobody asks for a Google review after the job.
>
> The question isn't whether clients will pay. The question is whether you're the one offering the solution -- or whether your competitor is.
>
> And the 'bigger agencies' you're scared of? Most of them are still using GoHighLevel and manually onboarding each client. They're slower than you. They're more expensive than you. They just look bigger.
>
> You don't need to be bigger. You need to be faster."

### 5. The Solution -- Agency Tier (60-90 sec)

> "This is why the Agency tier exists.
>
> Instead of selling hours, you sell a platform. Your brand, your domain, your pricing. Each client gets their own portal with AI agents handling conversations, booking appointments, capturing leads, requesting reviews -- 24/7.
>
> Agency gives you:
>
> - 2,000 credits per month for AI agent actions across all your clients
> - Sub-organizations -- each client fully isolated, your branding
> - White-label everything -- they never see l4yercak3
> - Custom domains -- their business name on the URL
> - 10,000 contacts, 10,000 emails per month
> - Template sharing -- configure once, deploy to every new client
> - Priority support -- 12-hour response
>
> Two sub-orgs are included. Additional clients are EUR 79/month each. Your typical client pays you EUR 199-349/month. Your cost per client: about EUR 10-20 in AI credits plus EUR 79 for the sub-org.
>
> That's 60-80% margin. On recurring revenue. That you set up once."

### 6. How It Works (45-60 sec)

> "Here's the workflow:
>
> Step one: Pick a vertical template -- salon, plumber, dental, restaurant, real estate. Or start from scratch.
>
> Step two: Create a sub-organization for your client. Upload their logo, set their colors, connect their domain. Upload their FAQ, service list, or menu. Set the AI agent's tone -- 'sound like the business owner.'
>
> Step three: Connect WhatsApp Business, set up booking, connect Stripe. Deploy.
>
> Your client logs in and sees their own branded portal. CRM, booking, analytics. Their customers interact with the AI agent on WhatsApp, web chat, and email. Leads get captured, appointments get booked, reviews get requested. Automatically.
>
> You configured it once. You charge monthly. You move to the next client.
>
> Agency is EUR 299 per month. Annual is EUR 2,990 -- two months free."

### 7. Who It's For / Not For (30-45 sec)

> "This is for agency owners who already have clients and want to deliver more value with less work. You don't need 50 clients. You need 5 who pay you EUR 299/month -- that's EUR 1,500/month recurring, and your platform cost is EUR 299.

> - People looking for a 'get rich quick' scheme -- this is a real business with real work
> - People who aren't willing to learn a new tool -- there's a learning curve, even though it's short"

### 8. CTA (30 sec)
