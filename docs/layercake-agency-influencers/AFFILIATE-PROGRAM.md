## Program Terms

### Cookie Duration
**90 days**

Why: Industry standard is 30-90 days. 90 days gives affiliates confidence that they'll get credit even for longer sales cycles.

### Attribution
**First-touch attribution**

Why: Affiliates get credit for introducing the lead, even if they convert later through other channels. This is affiliate-friendly.

### Payout Schedule
**Monthly, NET-30**

Payouts processed on the 1st of each month for previous month's commissions. 30-day hold for refund window.

### Minimum Payout
**$100**

Keeps it simple. Most affiliates will hit this quickly.

### Payment Methods
- PayPal
- Wise (for international)
- Direct bank transfer (US)
- Crypto (optional — differentiator)
