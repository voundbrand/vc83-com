
## C. The Sales Process

### C.1 From First Contact to Close

```
First Contact (BNI intro, DM reply, event meetup)
        ↓ 24 hours
Discovery Call (30 min — understand their business, pain, budget)
        ↓ 3-7 days
Live Birthing Demo (15 min — their AI comes alive, they feel it)
        ↓ 1-3 days
Proposal (tier recommendation based on needs + budget)
        ↓ 3-7 days
Decision Call (handle objections, close or negotiate)
        ↓ 1-7 days
Payment (50% upfront, 50% on delivery OR 100% upfront for Foundation)
        ↓ 1-4 weeks
Delivery + Onboarding
```

**Average sales cycle:**
- Foundation: 1-2 weeks
- Dream Team: 2-4 weeks
- Sovereign: 4-8 weeks

### C.2 Pricing Psychology

| Tactic | How to Use It |
|---|---|
| **Always present Sovereign first** | Anchor high. Even if they buy Foundation, they know there's more. |
| **Show the alternative cost** | "A marketing agency would charge €120K to build something half as good." |
| **The 15-minute birthing demo** | This is your close. Nobody competes with watching their AI come alive. |
| **"The AI remembers"** | Soul depth = switching cost. Explain this upfront: "In 3 months, leaving feels like firing a team member." |
| **Monthly fee as "insurance"** | "The €499/month is your AI's health insurance — updates, monitoring, and someone to call." |
| **Payment terms on Sovereign** | 50% upfront (€67,500). That first payment funds the next 3 Foundation clients. |

### C.3 Objection Handling (High-Ticket)

| Objection | Response |
|---|---|
| "€135K is a lot of money" | "How much does your team cost you per year? This replaces 3-5 roles and works 24/7. One captured lead at your average deal size pays for itself." |
| "Can't I just use ChatGPT?" | "ChatGPT forgets you every session. Seven Layers has 7 layers of memory that make it permanently yours. Try the birthing — you'll feel the difference in 15 minutes." |
| "Why do I need local hardware?" | "Your client data is your most valuable asset. With Sovereign, nothing ever leaves your building. No cloud. No third parties. Just your AI, on your desk." |
| "I need to think about it" | "Completely understand. Let me leave the AI we birthed running for a week — talk to it, see what it does. If it doesn't earn its keep in 7 days, no hard feelings." |
| "My IT person says they can build this" | "They probably could — in 6-12 months, for €100K+ in salary and infrastructure. I'll have it running on your desk in 2 days." |
| "We're not tech-savvy" | "That's exactly who this is for. You had a conversation with me. Your AI was born. That's the entire setup." |
