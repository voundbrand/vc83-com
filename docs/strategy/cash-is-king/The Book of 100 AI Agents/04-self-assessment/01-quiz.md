# Section 4 — Which Agents Do You Need?

## The Self-Assessment Quiz

---

### How This Works

Answer each question honestly. There are no wrong answers — only clarity about where your business needs the most help right now.

For each question, choose the answer that best describes your current situation. At the end, tally your scores to discover your **Agent Priority Profile**.
