## The Problem

80-90% of wealthy buyers have a "day one list" of trusted vendors. They buy from that list. If you're not on it, you don't exist.

Getting on the list requires social proof. Social proof requires clients. Clients require being on the list.

**The chicken-and-egg breaker: The Meta Glasses Live Demo.**

You have a working demo where AI agents connect to live video/audio feeds through Meta glasses and do real work in real-time. Nobody else has this. This isn't a pitch deck — it's a spectacle. It's the kind of thing people pull out their phones to record. The kind of thing they text their business partner about during the break.

That spectacle IS the trust-builder. It collapses the "prove it" cycle from months to minutes.

## The Weapon: Meta Glasses Live Demo

### Why This Changes Everything

Every other AI company shows slides. You put on glasses and your AI agents start working — live, visible, undeniable. The audience sees what they've only heard about in theory.

### Demo Scenarios (Pick 2-3 per event)

| Scenario | What the Audience Sees | "Holy Shit" Moment |
|---|---|---|
| **The Live Lead Capture** | You walk through the room. The glasses identify a person (with permission). The Closer agent drafts a personalized follow-up email in real-time based on the conversation it just heard. | The email appears on screen. It references things said 30 seconds ago. |
| **The Live Client Q&A** | Someone asks a complex business question. The agent pulls context from the business's knowledge base and answers through the glasses display — you read the answer out loud. | The agent knows the answer faster than any human team member could. |
| **The Operations Takeover** | Show a simulated incoming inquiry (WhatsApp message). The agent triages it, classifies urgency, drafts a response, and schedules a follow-up — all visible through the glasses in real-time. | 4 steps that normally take 15 minutes happen in 8 seconds. |
| **The Dream Team Meeting** | Trigger a team meeting between The Closer, The Strategist, and The CFO. They discuss a real business problem live. The audience watches AI specialists debate strategy in real-time. | Three AI personas with distinct voices collaborating — never seen before. |
| **The Birthing (Mini)** | In 5 minutes, birth a new agent for an audience volunteer's business. Their phone buzzes with a WhatsApp message from their new AI. | Their AI knows their business and speaks to them directly. Personal. Immediate. |

### Demo Rules

1. **Always live, never recorded.** The whole point is "this is happening right now." If something breaks, that's even better — it proves it's real.
2. **Always involve the audience.** Don't demo AT them. Demo WITH them. Use someone's real business. Answer someone's real question.
3. **Always end with the question:** "That took 15 minutes. Imagine what it does in 15 days."
4. **Film everything.** Every live demo becomes LinkedIn content. Every audience reaction becomes social proof.

### Phase 1: Warm — Your Existing Network (Week 1-4)

**Leverage: HIGHEST. Cost: ZERO. Start TODAY.**

These are people who already trust you. The demo just gives them a reason to invite you into their rooms.

#### Activity 1: The "Who Runs Events?" Blitz (Week 1)

Contact every person in your warm network and ask ONE question:

> "Hey [Name] — I just built something wild. AI agents that work through smart glasses in real-time. I'm looking for places to demo this live for business owners. Do you know anyone who organizes business events, runs a network, or leads a chapter of anything? BNI, IHK, Rotary, anything."

| Contact Source | Volume | Expected Intros |
|---|---|---|
| WhatsApp contacts (business-related) | 50-100 messages | 5-10 intros to event organizers |
| LinkedIn connections | 100-200 messages | 10-20 intros |
| Former colleagues | 20-30 messages | 3-5 intros |
| Friends/family who know business owners | 30-50 messages | 2-5 intros |

**Expected outcome:** 20-40 introductions to people who organize events, run chapters, or know someone who does.

#### Activity 2: The "Living Room Demo" (Week 2-4)

Before you pitch any organization, do 5-10 private demos for people in your network. Not sales — market research.

> "I'm refining this demo before I take it to events. Can I show you in 15 minutes and get your honest reaction? Coffee's on me."

| Purpose | How It Helps |
|---|---|
| Refine the demo flow | Which scenarios get the biggest reactions? |
| Collect testimonial clips | Film their reactions (with permission). "I've never seen anything like this." |
| Generate referrals | Every person who sees it will tell 3-5 others |
| Build confidence | By demo #10, you're bulletproof on stage |

#### Activity 3: Record and Post (Ongoing from Week 2)

Every demo you do → film it → post it on LinkedIn.

**LinkedIn posting cadence during Phase 1:**

| Day | Post Type |
|---|---|
| Monday | 60-second uncut demo clip: "Watch what happens when I put on the glasses." |
| Wednesday | Written post: one of the "21 Things That Disappear" stories. CTA: "Book link in comments." |
| Friday | Behind-the-scenes: building/testing/iterating. "Here's what I'm working on this week." |

**Goal:** By week 4, you have 5-10 reaction clips, 12+ LinkedIn posts, and a growing audience that sees you as "the AI glasses guy."

### Phase 2: Local Low-Barrier Events (Week 3-8)

**Leverage: HIGH. Cost: LOW. Fastest path to stage time.**

These organizations are easiest to get into and give you reps before the bigger rooms.

#### Target 1: BNI Chapters in MV + Surrounding Region

**Why first:** Lowest barrier. Weekly meetings. You can attend as a guest and request an "education slot" (5-10 min presentation). No formal application needed.

**How to get in:**
1. Find chapters: bni.com/country/germany → search Mecklenburg-Vorpommern, Rostock, Neubrandenburg, Schwerin, Berlin, Hamburg
2. Call the chapter and say: "Ich möchte als Gast teilnehmen. Ich habe eine KI-Demonstration mit Smart Glasses, die für Ihre Mitglieder interessant wäre."
3. Attend as guest → do the 5-min glasses demo during education slot
4. EVERY person in the room wants to know more

**Volume:** Attend 2-4 BNI chapters in 6 weeks. Each chapter = 25-40 members, each member knows 50-200+ people.

**Expected outcome:** 3-5 warm leads per chapter visit. Invitations to come back. Referrals to OTHER chapters.

#### Target 2: Wirtschaftsjunioren (Local Chapter)

**Why:** Young business owners (under 40), tech-friendly, easy to pitch, open to external speakers.

**How to get in:**
1. Search "Wirtschaftsjunioren [your nearest city]" — likely Neubrandenburg, Rostock, or Greifswald
2. Email: "Ich biete einen 30-minütigen Impulsvortrag mit Live-Demo: KI-Agenten mit Smart Glasses für den Mittelstand."
3. Include your speaker one-pager + 60-second video link

**Lead time:** 4-8 weeks (fast for Germany)

**Expected outcome:** 20-50 attendees. 5-10 warm leads. Video content for LinkedIn.

#### Target 3: Local Unternehmerverband / Unternehmerstammtisch

**Why:** Informal business owner meetups exist in every German region. Very low barrier. Often just need to ask.

**How to find them:**
- Search "Unternehmerstammtisch [your area]"
- Ask your IHK contact: "Gibt es lokale Unternehmerrunden, bei denen ich einen Kurzvortrag halten könnte?"
- Check Meetup.com for "Unternehmer" groups in MV

**Lead time:** 1-4 weeks

### Phase 3: IHK + Handwerkskammer (Week 4-12)

**Leverage: VERY HIGH. Cost: LOW. Medium timeline.**

IHK is your golden ticket to the Mittelstand. Every business owner in Germany interacts with their IHK.

#### IHK: The Exact Process

**Step 1: Call each MV chamber (Week 4)**

| Chamber | Phone | Ask For |
|---|---|---|
| **IHK Neubrandenburg** | 0395 5597-0 | "Referent für Digitalisierung" or "Veranstaltungsleiter" |
| **IHK Rostock** | 0381 338-0 | Same |
| **IHK Schwerin** | 0385 5103-0 | Same |

**What to say on the phone:**

> "Guten Tag, mein Name ist [Name], ich bin Gründer von Seven Layers aus [Stadt]. Wir entwickeln KI-Agenten für mittelständische Unternehmen. Ich habe eine Live-Demonstration mit Meta Smart Glasses, die für Ihre Digitalisierungs-Veranstaltungen hochinteressant wäre. Wer ist bei Ihnen für die Veranstaltungsplanung zuständig?"

**Goal:** Get the name and email of the event coordinator. Then send your speaker one-pager + video link.

**Step 2: Send the pitch email (same day)**

Subject: **Rednerangebot: KI-Agenten Live-Demo mit Smart Glasses für den Mittelstand**

Body: Speaker one-pager attached. Link to 3-minute sizzle reel. 3 talk title options. Your availability for the next 6 months.

**Step 3: Follow up (Week 7)**

Call back. Reference your email. Ask: "Haben Sie mein Angebot erhalten? Gibt es in den nächsten Monaten eine passende Veranstaltung?"

**Expected timeline:** 2-4 months to first IHK speaking slot. Some chambers move faster.

**Why IHK is gold:** One IHK event = 50-200 business owners in one room. Your exact ICP. They came because the IHK invited them — built-in trust transfer.

#### Handwerkskammer: Same Process, Different Angle

**The pitch twist for Handwerk:**

> "KI-Agenten für Handwerksbetriebe: Angebote automatisch nachfassen, Terminplanung, Kundenkommunikation — alles per WhatsApp, 24/7."

Handwerk owners don't care about "AI agents." They care about: "Will my phone stop ringing with the same questions? Will quotes get followed up automatically? Will I stop losing jobs because I was too slow to respond?"

**Target the Handwerkskammer events focused on:**
- Digitalisierung im Handwerk
- Fachkräftemangel (labor shortage) — AI as a force multiplier
- Betriebsführung (business management)

YPO is harder to penetrate directly. The play:

1. **Get a YPO member referral** from Vistage/EO speaking (someone in those rooms will know YPO members)

**Timeline:** 12-18 months. Don't rush this. Let it come from the other channels.

**Leverage: MULTIPLICATIVE. Cost: RELATIONSHIP TIME.**

Find 3-5 people who already have the trust of wealthy buyers. Arm them with your demo.

#### Who to Partner With

| Partner Type | Why They'll Say Yes | What They Get |
|---|---|---|
| **Unternehmensberater** (management consultants) | They advise €2M+ businesses on technology. They have no AI solution to recommend. You fill a gap in their offering. | 15-20% referral commission on every client they send |
| **Steuerberater / Wirtschaftsprüfer** (tax advisors / auditors) | They see the inside of every business's finances. They know who can afford €35K-195K. They want to add value beyond compliance. | Same commission structure |
| **IT-Systemhäuser** (IT service companies) | They manage business technology but don't do AI agents. Clients ask them about AI. They need an answer. | White-label option or referral fee |
| **Business coaches / Executive coaches** | They work 1:1 with CEOs. They recommend tools. An AI that amplifies their coaching? That's synergy. | Co-delivery model or referral fee |

#### The Partner Pitch

> "I build AI agents that do real business work — lead follow-up, client communication, operations. I have a live demo with Meta Smart Glasses that blows people's minds. Your clients are asking about AI. Right now you don't have an answer. I want to BE your answer. For every client you refer who closes, you earn 15-20% — that's €750 to €39,000 per referral. Want to see the demo?"

#### How to Find Them

1. **BNI chapters** — accountants, consultants, IT companies are ALWAYS in BNI. This is why BNI is Phase 2.
2. **LinkedIn search** — "Unternehmensberater [your region]"
3. **IHK directory** — every chamber has a member directory
4. **Steuerberater directory** — datev.de has advisor search
5. **Ask your first clients:** "Who advises you on technology? Who does your taxes? Can I meet them?"

**Target:** 3-5 active referral partners by month 6. Each partner sends 1-2 referrals per quarter = 3-10 referrals per quarter from partnerships alone.

## The Chicken-and-Egg Solved

Here's how the chicken-and-egg breaks, step by step:

**Week 1-4:** You don't need case studies. You have the Meta glasses. The spectacle IS the credibility. Nobody asks "who are your clients?" when they've just watched an AI agent draft a personalized email through smart glasses in real-time. They ask "how do I get this?"

**Week 5-8:** Your first 2-3 BNI/Wirtschaftsjunioren demos generate your first clients (likely Foundation tier, €5K). Small, fast closes. Now you have case studies.

**Week 9-16:** IHK events put you in front of 50-200 Mittelstand owners. You have the demo AND the case studies. Dream Team (€35K) conversations start.

**Week 17-24:** Vistage/EO talks put you in front of €5M+ CEOs. You have the demo, the case studies, the IHK speaking record, AND LinkedIn proof. Sovereign (€135K) conversations start.

**Month 7-12:** You're on the day one list. Event organizers call YOU. Partners send YOU referrals. LinkedIn audience sends YOU inbound leads. The flywheel is spinning.

The Meta glasses demo is the key that unlocks every door. It's the thing nobody can replicate, nobody can ignore, and nobody forgets. Every other AI company shows PowerPoint. You show the future — live, through your eyes.
