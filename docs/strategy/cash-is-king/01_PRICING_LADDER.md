# Document 01: The Pricing Ladder

**Seven Layers | Cash-Is-King Strategy**
**Date:** 2026-02-23
**Classification:** Internal — Founder's Eyes Only
**Purpose:** The Hormozi-style high-ticket pricing architecture. Every tier costed, margined, and positioned.

---

## Why This Replaces the Old Model

The original strategy (Docs 00-04) projected a PLG self-serve SaaS at €49-399/month, requiring 748 paying customers to reach €673K ARR by month 12. That model requires:
- A content engine running at full speed from day 1
- Thousands of signups to convert 3-4% to paid
- Zero runway to survive the 8-month burn to break-even
- Competing for $2 among the bottom 50% (Hormozi's framing)

**The new model:** Sell to the rich. Start at the top. Work down. 18 high-ticket clients = same or more revenue than 748 self-serve users. Zero content engine required. Zero paid ads. Zero hires needed in year 1.

**Source:** Alex Hormozi's pricing framework — 5-10x jumps between tiers, expect 20% uptake at each higher level, start as high as you can.

---

## The Five-Tier Ladder

```
€195K ──── Sovereign Max    (Mac Studio 512GB — run anything)
€165K ──── Sovereign Pro    (DGX Spark + Mac Studio cluster)
€135K ──── Sovereign        (Mac Studio 256GB — your hardware, your data)
 €35K ──── Dream Team       (dedicated cloud — full AI team)
  €5K ──── Foundation       (shared cloud — your AI is born)
```

Each tier includes a setup fee (one-time) and a monthly platform fee (recurring).
