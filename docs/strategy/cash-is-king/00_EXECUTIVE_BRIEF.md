**Stop competing for €49/month from people who don't have money. Sell €5K-195K packages to business owners who do — through BNI, direct outreach, and live demos that make their AI come alive in 15 minutes.**

To prevent cognitive dissonance between marketing and product behavior, all public launch/demo copy must follow the FOG2 onboarding entry contract:

1. Public CTA URL (current live demo domain): `https://app.l4yercak3.com/` (root URL).

3. Root entry opens AI chat first; beta code capture and signup happen conversationally in-chat.
4. `app.sevenlayers.io` remains a future cutover domain and must keep the same root-entry behavior when activated.

## The 3 Things That Must Be True

1. **The birthing demo must close deals.** If someone watches their AI come alive in 15 minutes and doesn't feel "I need this," the pricing doesn't matter. The demo is the close.

2. **BNI must produce referrals.** If the chapter doesn't generate warm intros within 3 months, pivot time investment to direct outreach and events.

3. **Sovereign clients must be referenceable.** One case study from a €135K client is worth more than 100 testimonials from €49/month users. Every Sovereign client must become a reference.

## The Year 2 Play (Tesla Model 3)

Once Year 1 profits fund the machine:
- Hire content person (€40-55K/year)
- Launch self-serve tiers (€49-399/month)
- Turn on the PLG content engine
- **The mass-market product, funded by high-ticket profits**

This is how Tesla did it. Roadster → Model S → Model 3. You don't start at Model 3. You earn the right to serve the masses by first serving the few who can afford to pay.
