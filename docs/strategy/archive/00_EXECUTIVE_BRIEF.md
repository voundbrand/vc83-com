## The 3 Things That Must Be True

For this strategy to work, three things must be true:

1. **The birthing experience must create emotional attachment in 15 minutes.** If users don't feel "this is MY AI" after birthing, nothing else matters.

2. **The agent must deliver tangible value in the first week.** Book an appointment. Draft an email. Follow up with a lead. Something real, not a demo.

3. **Soul depth must create genuine switching cost.** After 3 months, leaving must feel like losing a team member, not canceling a subscription.

Everything in the product roadmap, content strategy, and pricing architecture serves these three truths.
