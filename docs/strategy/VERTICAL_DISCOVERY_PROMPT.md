## PROMPT — COPY FROM HERE

You are a brutally honest market strategist. I'm a solo founder in Germany with a fully built AI operations platform (520K lines of code, 3-4 years of engineering). I need to find the right vertical to hit **EUR 100K monthly recurring revenue** as fast as possible.

### WHAT I'VE ALREADY BUILT (this is real, production-ready)

**AI Agent Layer:**
- 7 specialized AI agents (receptionist, appointment coordinator, lead qualification, field documentation, customer follow-up, team operations, location intelligence)
- 43 AI tools the agents can execute autonomously (CRM, booking, invoicing, forms, workflows, media, code execution, project management)
- Voice runtime (ElevenLabs) — agents answer phone calls naturally in German and English
- Memory system (session context, contact history, pinned notes, RAG knowledge base)
- Autonomy levels (supervised → autonomous) with human-in-the-loop escalation
- Self-improving agents (soul evolution — agents propose their own behavior changes)

**Operational Platform:**
- Full CRM with contact/organization management
- Booking & scheduling engine with real-time availability, multi-resource support, recurring bookings
- Google Calendar + Microsoft Calendar sync (read/write)
- Invoicing system (B2B consolidated + B2C, payment terms, deposits, VAT)
- Stripe payment integration + checkout pages
- Event management with ticketing
- Forms builder with conditional logic
- Email + SMS + WhatsApp + Telegram multi-channel
- DAG-based visual workflow automation engine (better than n8n architecturally)
- Web page builder + CMS with custom domain support
- Multi-tenant with RBAC (role-based access control)
- Full API + webhooks

**What makes this unique:** Everything runs on ONE polymorphic data model. The AI agents can operate across ALL business functions simultaneously — book an appointment, create an invoice, send a follow-up, update the CRM, trigger a workflow — in a single conversation. No one else has this integrated stack.

**My proven demo weapon:** A phone number someone can call. The AI agent answers, handles the conversation naturally, and takes real actions (books appointments, creates tickets, qualifies leads, routes to the right person). Decision-makers hear it and go "holy shit." This demo works in under 60 seconds.

### MY CONSTRAINTS (be realistic about these)

- **Solo founder** — I am the salesperson, the implementer, and the product developer
- **Based in Germany** — fluent German + English, timezone advantage for DACH
- **Need cash flow NOW** — target is EUR 100K MRR within 12 months
- **No VC funding** — bootstrapped, need positive unit economics from customer #1
- **My sales motion works:** 30-min demo call → demo kit for internal buy-in → 2-week free trial → close on trial data
- **My setup capacity:** I can onboard ~2-3 new customers per month while maintaining existing ones
- **Price range:** EUR 2K-10K/month recurring per customer (depending on scope)

### WHAT I'VE LEARNED (from deep research into FM, MVZ, and general SMB)

**Vertical killers for me:**
1. **Margins below 10%** — buyer can't justify the spend (FM has 3-7% margins = too tight)
2. **Sales cycles over 3 months** — I need cash flow, not a 12-month enterprise pipeline
3. **Existing AI phone competitors** in the vertical (Doctolib/Aaron for MVZ, MANAGBL.AI for Hausverwaltung)
4. **Commoditized phone-only value prop** — if HalloPetra at EUR 199/mo covers 80% of the need, I can't charge EUR 5K/mo
5. **Buyer needs committee approval** — works councils, procurement departments, legal review = death for a solo founder
6. **No phone volume** — if the business doesn't get inbound calls, my demo weapon doesn't work

**Vertical accelerators for me:**
1. **Phone is the revenue front door** — missed calls = missed money (visceral demo)
2. **Multi-location operations** — one deal, many locations, big MRR
3. **Owner-operator decision maker** — one person says yes, deal is done
4. **15%+ margins** — buyer has room to invest in operations
5. **Currently on WhatsApp/Excel/paper** — no incumbent software to displace
6. **Needs MORE than just phone** — booking + CRM + invoicing + follow-up = sticky platform
7. **Repeat/recurring customer relationships** — ongoing operational value, not one-time projects
8. **German Mittelstand DNA** — family-owned, 50-500 employees, values reliability and Gründlichkeit

### THE MATH I NEED

EUR 100K MRR = one of these:
- 10 customers × EUR 10K/month
- 20 customers × EUR 5K/month
- 33 customers × EUR 3K/month

At 2-3 new customers per month, I need:
- 10 customers: 4-5 months to reach target
- 20 customers: 7-10 months
- 33 customers: 11-16 months

**Sweet spot: 10-20 customers at EUR 5-10K/month = reach EUR 100K MRR in 6-10 months.**
