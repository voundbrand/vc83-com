## Channel 1: ElevenLabs Partnership

### Current status
- Already building on ElevenLabs. 7 AI agents with multi-agent handoff, star topology, 10+ regression scenarios.
- ElevenLabs has 10 commercial partners — ALL US/UK/Eastern Europe. Zero DACH. Zero legal vertical.
- $11B valuation, $330M ARR. They want partners who bring markets they can't reach from San Francisco.

### What we bring to ElevenLabs
- **Technical credential:** Most sophisticated German-language multi-agent voice system on their platform
- **Market credential:** 100K+ DACH professional services businesses mapped (5K law firms, 50K Steuerberater, 10K vets)
- **Revenue credential:** Each law firm deployment = $50-200/month in ElevenLabs platform usage
- **Geographic credential:** First credible DACH partner with vertical expertise

### What we get from ElevenLabs
- Partner credits for PoC builds
- Direct support channel (faster response than community)
- Co-marketing (blog post, case study, partner directory listing)
- Potential lead referrals (enterprise inquiries that need DACH implementation)
- Partner badge for credibility on landing page

### What we will NOT get
- Exclusivity (ElevenLabs takes any credible partner in any region)
- Leads for law firms (they don't generate Kanzlei leads)
- Revenue share on usage (affiliate program is separate: 22% for 12 months on plan signups)
- Dedicated partner manager (we're too small initially)

## Channel 3: RA-MICRO Partnership (Long-Term Moat)

### Current reality (honest)

- RA-MICRO has 70K workplaces. Market leader in Kanzleisoftware.
- They have ~20 cooperation partners (Wolters Kluwer, SCHUFA, e.Consult, etc.)
- **No public API.** Interfaces are legacy file exports (DATEV, ELSTER, Excel).
- **No marketplace.** No developer portal. No SDK.
- **Hallo Kira lists "RA-MICRO (coming soon)"** — they're ahead on this conversation.

### Integration path

| Phase | What | How | Timeline |
|-------|------|-----|----------|
| **1 (Now)** | Calendar + email only | Google/Outlook Calendar sync, structured email to Kanzlei inbox | Day 1 |
| **2 (Month 3-6)** | RA-MICRO data import | Generate RA-MICRO-compatible CSV/XML for contact import | Month 3 |
| **3 (Month 6-12)** | Direct partnership | Contact RA-MICRO with 5-10 deployed Kanzleien as proof. Negotiate API access. | Month 6+ |
| **4 (Year 2)** | Deep integration | Bidirectional: read from RA-MICRO (Akte, Kalender), write to RA-MICRO (neue Mandate, Termine) | Year 2 |

### Who to contact at RA-MICRO

- RA-MICRO GmbH & Co. KG, Tauentzienstr. 9-12, 10789 Berlin
- General contact: ra-micro.de/service/kontakt/jetzt-informieren/kontaktformular.html
- Cooperation partners page: ra-micro.de/unternehmen/ueber-ra-micro/kooperationspartner.html
- **Don't approach until we have 5+ deployed law firms.** They get pitched constantly by single-feature startups.

### The RA-MICRO risk

- **Non-exclusive by pattern.** They list multiple partners per category (3+ credit agencies, 2+ publishers). Getting in doesn't lock Kira out — and vice versa.
- **The real moat is not the partnership. It's the depth of integration.** Whoever writes the deepest RA-MICRO integration (bidirectional Aktenanlage, automatic Fristenberechnung) wins the stickiest customers.
