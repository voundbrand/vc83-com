## Why Law Firms Won the Vertical Scorecard

From a weighted assessment of 23 verticals across TAM, margins, ARPU, expansion potential, integration moat, competition, and speed to close:

### The Nuclear Sales Stat

> **59% of callers hang up on voicemail. 68% of those NEVER call back. They call the next lawyer.**

This stat does three things in a sales conversation:
1. Creates visceral pain recognition ("that's us")
2. Quantifies the revenue leak ("how many calls went to voicemail this week?")
3. Makes the solution self-evident ("what if every call was answered?")

## Priority Practice Areas (within law firms)

Not all law firms are equal. Priority practice areas for Phase 0:

| Practice Area | Why It's First | Urgency Driver | Template Reuse |
|---------------|---------------|----------------|----------------|
| **Arbeitsrecht** (Labor law) | 3-Wochen-Frist for Kündigungsschutzklage creates EXTREME time pressure. Caller who can't reach a lawyer in 24h may lose their case. | Statutory deadline | Very high (same questions: Kündigung? Frist? Arbeitgeber? Betriebszugehörigkeit?) |
| **Familienrecht** (Family law) | Emotional callers, high volume, Trennungsjahr creates urgency. Often first contact with any lawyer. | Emotional urgency + life event | High (same questions: Trennung? Kinder? Vermögen? Unterhalt?) |
| **Mietrecht** (Tenancy law) | High volume, frequent disputes, relatively standardized intake. | Frist bei Kündigung, Mietminderung | High |
| **Strafrecht** (Criminal law) | Urgent by nature (arrest, charges). Often after-hours calls. | Legal emergency | Moderate (wide variety of cases) |

**Avoid initially:** Wirtschaftsrecht (corporate/commercial) — long sales cycles, complex intake. Patentrecht — niche, low volume.

## Buyer Persona: The Managing Partner

| Dimension | Detail |
|-----------|--------|
| **Title** | Kanzleiinhaber, geschäftsführender Partner, Senior Partner |
| **Age** | 45-60 typically |
| **Tech comfort** | Low-medium. Uses RA-MICRO because they have to. iPhone yes, cloud-skeptical. |
| **Pain** | "Ich weiß, dass wir Mandate verlieren, weil niemand ans Telefon geht. Aber ich kann nicht noch eine Mitarbeiterin einstellen." |
| **Budget authority** | Full. No procurement committee. No board approval. |
| **Decision speed** | Fast once convinced. "Zeigen Sie mir, dass es funktioniert, und wir machen das." |
| **Trust signals** | BRAO-compliant, DSGVO, "Made in Germany", reference from another Kanzlei |
| **Objection** | "Meine Mandanten wollen mit einem Menschen sprechen." → Reframe: "Kira nimmt auf, qualifiziert, und bucht — kein Mandant wartet mehr in der Warteschleife." |

## Disqualification Criteria

| Signal | Action |
|--------|--------|
| Solo practitioner (Einzelanwalt) | Too small — monthly COGS may exceed their willingness to pay. Redirect to Hallo Kira. |
| 50+ lawyers | Too large — procurement, IT department, long sales cycle. Revisit in Year 2. |
| No phone volume (<10 calls/day) | No pain, no sale. |
| Already has Hallo Kira and is happy | Move on. Don't fight entrenched competitor for one customer. |
| "We need RA-MICRO integration before we start" | Defer — integration not available yet. Focus on firms willing to start with calendar + email. |
| Practice area with no time pressure | Lower urgency = longer sales cycle. Prioritize Arbeitsrecht/Familienrecht. |

> If they don't miss calls and don't lose Mandate because of it, we don't have a sale. Move on.
