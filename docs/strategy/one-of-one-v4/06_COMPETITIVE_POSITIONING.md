## Category Creation

> We are not a "KI-Telefonassistent." We are an **AI Kanzleiassistenz** — the difference between someone who answers your phone and someone who runs your Mandatsannahme.

### The category map

| Category | Players | What They Do | Price Range |
|----------|---------|-------------|-------------|
| **Telefonsekretariat** (human) | ebuero, Anwaltssekretariat, starbuero | Human takes message, emails summary | €60-180/mo + per call |
| **KI-Telefonassistent** (AI phone) | Hallo Kira, HalloPetra | AI answers phone, basic qualification | €99-500/mo |
| **Legal AI Intake** (text/web) | JUPUS | Web chatbot, document AI, intake forms | Unknown (enterprise) |
| **AI Call Automation** (high-volume) | Telli | AI voice for high-volume B2C call ops | Enterprise pricing |
| **AI Kanzleiassistenz** (us) | One-of-One | AI qualification + booking + CRM + practice area routing + dashboard | €499-1,999/mo |

**The positioning:** We sit above the phone assistants (more value) and below a full hire (less cost). We're the only one that qualifies, books, and writes to the firm's systems.

### How we win against Hallo Kira

**Not on price.** They're 3-5x cheaper. Don't fight this battle.

**Win on outcomes:**

| Kira does | We do |
|-----------|-------|
| Answers the phone | Answers, qualifies by practice area, assesses urgency |
| Takes a message | Generates structured intake summary for the assigned lawyer |
| "Wir melden uns" | Books the Erstberatung directly in the right lawyer's calendar |
| Sends email | Sends email + creates CRM entry + updates dashboard |
| Counts calls | Shows conversion funnel: calls → qualified → booked → appeared |

**The one-liner:**

> "Hallo Kira beantwortet Ihr Telefon. Wir betreiben Ihre Mandatsannahme."

### Hallo Kira risk assessment

| Risk | Probability | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|------------|--------|-----------|
| Kira ships RA-MICRO integration first | HIGH | MEDIUM | Don't depend on RA-MICRO moat. Win on outcome depth. |
| Kira adds booking | MEDIUM | HIGH | They have basic booking. Our lawyer-matched, practice-area-routed booking is deeper. |
| Kira adds dashboard | MEDIUM | MEDIUM | Dashboard is a feature, not a moat. Our data depth (qualification, urgency, conversion) is harder to replicate. |
| Kira drops price to €49/mo | LOW | LOW | We don't compete on price. Different category. |
| Kira raises €5M+ | MEDIUM | HIGH | Accelerates all above risks. We need 12 months of head start on outcome depth. |

## Competitor 2: JUPUS (Adjacent Threat)

| Dimension | JUPUS | Us |
|-----------|-------|-----|
| **What** | AI software for law firms — automated client intake via web | AI Kanzleiassistenz — phone-first intake |
| **Founded** | Cologne, René Fergen & Jannis Gebauer | Solo founder |
| **Funding** | **€6.5M seed** (Acton Capital, HTGF) | Bootstrapped |
| **Focus** | Web chatbot, document AI, online forms | Phone calls, voice qualification, calendar booking |
| **Channel** | Website visitor → chatbot → intake form | Phone caller → AI agent → booked appointment |
| **Metric** | "40 working hours saved per month" | "€X,000 in recovered Mandate per month" |

### How we coexist (or compete)

JUPUS handles **web intake**. We handle **phone intake**. These are complementary — a Kanzlei could use both.

**Threat scenario:** JUPUS adds phone. With €6.5M in funding, they could. But their DNA is text/web, not voice. Building a multi-agent voice system is a different engineering challenge. We have 12-18 months before they'd ship something competitive.

**Our advantage:** The managing partner's most painful channel is the phone, not the website. "59% hang up on voicemail" doesn't apply to web chatbots.

## Competitor 4: Telli (Adjacent, Future Threat)

| Dimension | Telli | Us |
|-----------|-------|-----|
| **What** | AI voice agents for high-volume B2C call operations | AI Kanzleiassistenz for professional services |
| **Funding** | €3.1M (Cherry Ventures, YC) | Bootstrapped |
| **Customers** | McMakler, Homeday, Zenjob, Enpal | Law firms (Phase 0) |
| **Sweet spot** | 100+ employees, 1,000+ calls/day | 5-20 lawyers, 20-80 calls/day |
| **Vertical** | Real estate, solar, staffing, healthcare | Legal, then Steuerberater, Vet, etc. |

### Why Telli is not an immediate threat

1. Their ICP is tech-forward companies with engineering teams. A 5-partner Kanzlei is not their customer.
2. Their pricing model is enterprise/high-volume. Law firm economics don't fit.
3. They have no legal domain expertise (BRAO, practice areas, Fristen).
4. Their expansion roadmap focuses on their existing verticals, not entering legal.

**Watch for:** If Telli raises Series A and announces "professional services expansion," we have 12-18 months to establish our position.

## Competitive Selling Rules

1. **Never badmouth Hallo Kira.** If the prospect mentions them: "Gute Lösung für Telefonannahme. Wir machen etwas anderes — Mandatsannahme."
2. **Never compete on price.** If they want €99/month, point them to Hallo Kira. Seriously. They're not our customer.
3. **Always demo, never slide-deck.** Our product sells itself in a 3-minute phone call. No competitor can match the demo experience.
4. **Lead with the call audit.** Free data beats any pricing argument. Show them what they're losing today.
5. **Frame as investment, not expense.** "€499/Monat für €3,000/Monat an neuen Mandaten" is a 6x ROI story.
