## Phase 0 Pricing Philosophy

> "The audit is free because it IS the sales tool. The pilot is cheap because we need proof, not profit. The conversion is fair because the data justifies the price."

---

## Three-Step Price Ladder

| Step | Price | Duration | What They Get | Why This Price |
|------|-------|----------|---------------|----------------|
| **1. Call Audit** | €0 | 2 weeks | Overflow calls captured, qualified, summarized. Audit report with revenue-at-risk math. | Free removes all friction. The data creates urgency that justifies Step 2. |
| **2. Pilot** | €299/month | 2 weeks | Full intake: qualification + calendar booking + email notifications. All 3 agents active. | Low enough to say yes without a meeting. High enough to signal commitment. |
| **3. Paid** | €499-999/month | Ongoing | Full service. Monthly optimization. Dashboard access. Practice area templates. | See 05_PRICING_V4.md for full tier details. |

### Setup fees during Phase 0

| Step | Setup Fee | Rationale |
|------|-----------|-----------|
| Call Audit | €0 | No barrier. |
| Pilot | €0 | Still in proof mode. Don't charge for setup during pilot. |
| Paid (Basis) | €1,000 (reduced from €1,500) | Phase 0 discount. Covers real work but reduces friction. |
| Paid (Professional) | €1,500 (reduced from €2,000) | Phase 0 discount. |

**Phase 0 setup discounts end when we reach 10 paying customers.** After that, full v4 pricing applies.

## Why These Prices

### vs. Hallo Kira

| | Hallo Kira | Our Phase 0 |
|-|-----------|-------------|
| Entry | €99/month (100 min included) | €0 (call audit) |
| Full service | €169-299/month | €499-999/month |
| What they get | Phone answering + basic qualification | Phone + deep qualification + booking + structured intake + dashboard |

**We are 2-3x Hallo Kira's price.** This is intentional. We deliver categorically more value. But we need the call audit to PROVE that value before asking for the premium.

### vs. the "do nothing" alternative

| | Doing Nothing | Our Service |
|-|--------------|-------------|
| Cost | €0/month | €499-999/month |
| Revenue lost | €3,000-5,000/month (estimated from audit data) | Recovered by our agents |
| ROI | -€3,000-5,000/month | +€2,000-4,000/month net |

### vs. hiring

| | Part-time Sekretärin | Our Service |
|-|---------------------|-------------|
| Cost | €1,500-2,500/month (incl. Sozialabgaben) | €499-999/month |
| Hours | 20h/week, Mon-Fri | 24/7/365 |
| Sick days | Yes | No |
| Vacation | Yes (30 days/year) | No |
| Training | Ongoing | Included |
| Qualification depth | Depends on person | Consistent, improving |

## Discounting Rules

| Rule | Detail |
|------|--------|
| Never discount below €499/month for Basis | Floor. If they can't afford €499, point them to Hallo Kira. |
| Never waive setup fee for paid conversions | Even at Phase 0 reduced rate. Signals commitment. |
| Extend audit beyond 2 weeks if needed | Low cost. If volume is low, 3-4 weeks gives better data. |
| Extend pilot beyond 2 weeks if needed | At €299/month, extra weeks are cheap. Better to extend than lose the conversion. |
| Offer 3-month commitment for 10% discount | Only if they ask. Don't lead with it. €449/month for Basis on 3-month term. |
