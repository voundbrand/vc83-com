## The One-Sentence Strategy

> Build the vertical AI phone + intake platform for German law firms, powered by ElevenLabs, with our own CRM/booking/dashboard as the moat. Start with Kanzleien. Own the category. Expand to all DACH professional services.

## 30-Second Founder Pitch

> "59% of callers hang up on law firm voicemail. 68% of those never call back — they call the next lawyer. We give Kanzleien an AI receptionist that answers every call, qualifies the Mandatsanfrage by practice area and urgency, and books the Erstberatung directly into the lawyer's calendar. It runs 24/7, speaks fluent German, and costs less than a part-time Sekretärin. We start with the phone because it proves itself in one call. Then we expand into the rest of the practice operation."

## Why We Pivoted

| | v3 (Mid-Market Horizontal) | v4 (Vertical Law Firm SaaS) |
|---|---------------------------|----------------------------|
| **ICP** | 50-500 employees, €5M-€50M revenue, 3-20 locations | 5-20 lawyers, €1M-€5M revenue, 1-3 locations |
| **Entry product** | 2-week trial converting to €35K+ Dream Team | Free call audit converting to €499-1,999/month |
| **Revenue target** | 10-15 customers × €35K-€195K = €1M-€1.5M in 12-18mo | 30-50 customers × €750 avg/mo = €22-38K MRR in 12mo |
| **Sales motion** | Demo → Demo Kit → Trial → Close | Call Audit → Pilot → Paid → Expand |
| **Vertical** | MVZ, real estate, Mittelstand (none owned) | Law firms (owned), then Steuerberater, Vet, etc. |
| **Positioning** | "Intelligent front door for the business" | "AI Kanzleiassistenz — die Sekretärin, die nie krank ist" |
| **Exit path** | Enterprise SaaS (€50M valuation at 10x) | Platform company: vertical SaaS for DACH professional services |
| **ElevenLabs role** | Infrastructure provider | Infrastructure provider (same, but we own the customer) |
| **Pricing anchor** | vs. call centers, vs. hiring ops staff | vs. Sekretärin (€1,500-2,500/mo), vs. Hallo Kira (€99-299/mo) |

### Why the pivot

1. **v3 mid-market deals don't close without proof.** No law firm managing partner writes a €35K check to a solo founder with zero deployed customers. The €35K-€195K pricing requires a sales team, case studies, and enterprise credibility we don't have yet.

2. **The vertical opportunity is larger than the horizontal.** 5,000+ mid-size Kanzleien in Germany alone, plus 50,000+ Steuerberater, 10,000+ Tierarztpraxen. One vertical done right opens a 100K+ business market across DACH professional services.

3. **A direct competitor validated the market.** Hallo Kira exists, is live, charges €99-299/month for AI phone at law firms. The market is real. But they're a point solution — phone answering only. We're building the platform.

4. **The platform already exists.** 520K lines of code: CRM, booking engine, invoicing, calendar sync, multi-tenant RBAC, agent orchestration. It's not wired to the voice agents yet. Wiring it is the competitive moat.

5. **Solo founder bootstrapped economics.** v3 needs 0.5-1 deals/month at €35K+. v4 needs 3-5 deals/month at €500-1,000. The latter is achievable through warm outreach, events, and referrals. The former requires a sales pipeline we can't build alone.

| **Primary deal size** | €35K-€195K setup + €999-€10K/mo | €1,500-2,500 setup + €499-1,999/mo |

| **Vertical** | Horizontal (MVZ, RE, Mittelstand) | Vertical (law firms → Steuerberater → expand) |
| **Competitive frame** | vs. Logicc, vs. horizontal AI workspace | vs. Hallo Kira, vs. Sekretärin, vs. ebuero |
| **Channel** | LinkedIn, BNI, conferences | Legal associations, RA-MICRO, ElevenLabs partnership |
| **ElevenLabs** | Infrastructure | Infrastructure + partnership (DACH partner positioning) |
| **Platform** | Selling 7-agent platform | Selling outcomes (calls answered, Mandate gewonnen) |

## What Stayed

- **Founder-led sales.** The founder demos, configures, and closes.
- **Phone agent as wedge.** "Call this number" remains the most powerful sales tool.
- **"Give away the audit, sell the implementation."** The call audit is free. The product earns its price.
- **The platform itself.** Multi-agent, multi-channel, CRM, booking, calendar — unchanged.
- **GDPR-first.** German hosting, EU data residency, BRAO-compliant agent behavior.
- **ElevenLabs voice.** 7 agents with multi-handoff, tested with 10+ regression scenarios.

## Revenue Target (Year 1 — Bootstrapped)

| Metric | Target |
|--------|--------|
| **Total law firm customers** | 30-50 |
| **Average MRR per customer** | €750 |
| **MRR by month 6** | €5-7K |
| **MRR by month 12** | €22-38K |
| **Year 1 total revenue** | €150-250K |
| **Gross margin** | 40-60% (improves with templates) |

### The math

- 3-5 new customers/month at €750 avg = €2.25-3.75K incremental MRR/month
- Solo ceiling at 12-15 active customers → first hire at month 6-8
- With first hire: capacity doubles to 25-30 customers
- Break-even (covers founder + one hire) at ~€15K MRR = 20 customers
- Steuerberater expansion begins at month 6-9, adds second growth curve

### Path to platform company

- Year 1: €150-250K revenue, 30-50 law firms, proven templates
- Year 2: €500K-€1M revenue, 80-150 firms (law + Steuerberater), RA-MICRO integration
- Year 3: €1-2M revenue, 200+ firms, 3-4 verticals, potential raise from strength
- Year 4+: €3-5M ARR, own category ("AI Kanzleiassistenz"), acquisition target or growth equity
