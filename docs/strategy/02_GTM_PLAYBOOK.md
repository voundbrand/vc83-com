### B.3 Conversion Optimization

**The funnel:**

```
Visit sevenlayers.io → Start Free Birthing → Complete Birthing → First Chat
         ↓                    ↓                     ↓                ↓
      100%               → 40%              → 85%           → 70%
                    (start birthing)    (finish birthing)  (chat within 24h)
```

```
First Chat → 3 Chats in 7 Days → Hit Message Limit → Upgrade
    ↓               ↓                    ↓               ↓
  70%          → 60%              → 50%           → 30%
          (activation)        (limit reached)    (conversion)
```

**Target end-to-end:** 100 visitors → 40 start birthing → 34 complete → 24 activate → 12 hit limit → 3-4 convert to paid.

**Conversion rate from visit to paid: ~3-4%** (industry avg for PLG SaaS: 2-5%)

### B.4 Retention from Day 1

| Touchpoint | Timing | Mechanism |
|-----------|--------|-----------|
| Welcome message from agent | Immediately after birthing | "I'm [Name], born from our conversation. Here's what I know about your business..." |
| First proactive suggestion | Within 24 hours | Agent identifies something it can help with based on birthing data |
| "Day 3" engagement nudge | Day 3 if no chat | Agent reaches out: "Hey [Name], I noticed [business insight]. Want to talk about it?" |
| Weekly soul report | Every Monday | "Here's what I learned about your business this week" |
| Message limit warning | At 80% of limit | "You've used 40 of your 50 messages. Upgrade to keep the conversation going." |

### C.3 Referral Program Launch

| Mechanism | Reward |
|-----------|--------|
| Free refers free | Both get 100 extra messages |
| Free refers paid | Referrer gets 1 month Starter free |
| Paid refers paid | Both get 1 month credit |
| Agent-to-agent | Your AI suggests contacts it interacts with should get their own AI |

**Target viral coefficient: >0.3** (each user brings 0.3 new users)

### C.4 Agency Referral Program

Agencies shift from customer to channel partner:

| Model | How It Works | Agency Earns |
|-------|-------------|-------------|
| **Referral** | Agency sends client, client self-onboards | 20% lifetime commission |
| **Setup partner** | Agency runs premium birthing (deeper interview) | €500-2K setup fee + 15% recurring |
| **Managed service** | Agency manages ongoing agent optimization | Their service fee + 10% recurring |
| **White-label** | Agency brands the agent as their own | €99/mo white-label add-on |

**Founding partners (first 10):** 25% lifetime commission (vs. standard 20%).

**Target:** 15-20 agency partners by month 12.

## F. Metrics Dashboard

### F.1 Weekly Check (Every Monday)

| Metric | Target | How to Measure |
|--------|--------|---------------|
| New signups | Growing week-over-week | Signup events |
| Birthing completions | >85% of signups who start | Funnel analytics |
| Activation rate | >60% (3+ chats in 7 days) | Chat event tracking |
| WAA (Weekly Active Agents) | Growing | Agent activity logs |
| Free → Paid conversion | >15% within 30 days | Stripe + funnel |
| MRR | Track against plan | Stripe dashboard |

### F.2 Monthly Check

| Metric | Target | Notes |
|--------|--------|-------|
| Churn (paying) | <5% monthly → <3% at month 12+ | Soul depth reduces churn over time |
| NPS | >50 | Survey at day 30 and day 90 |
| LTV:CAC | >15x | Review quarterly |
| Net Revenue Retention | >110% | Upgrades + add-ons should exceed churn revenue |
| Content output | 8 YouTube + 20 LinkedIn + 8 blog posts | Track weekly |

### F.3 Quarterly Review

| Question | What to Look At |
|---------|----------------|
| Is the birthing flow converting? | Completion rate, time to complete, drop-off points |
| Are users forming the daily habit? | Messages per active user per week (target: >20) |
| What triggers upgrades? | Most common upgrade event (message limit? channel need? team member?) |
| Is the Dream Team being discovered and expanded? | % of users who activate direct/meeting mode by month 3 + # of templates adopted per user |
| What's the word-of-mouth quality? | How are users describing Seven Layers to others? |
