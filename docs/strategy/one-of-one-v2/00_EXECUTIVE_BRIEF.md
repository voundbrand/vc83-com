**Give away a live AI diagnostic that proves we're the best, then sell €7,000–€34,999 implementations to millionaire business owners — with modular infrastructure add-ons that expand client value from €25K to €300K+ lifetime, anchored against a €195,000 Sovereign tier that delivers a full private AI platform.**

## What We Sell

**One operator. Yours alone. Running wherever you need it.**

A personal AI operator that is born from a conversation about your business. It lives on WhatsApp, web, voice, email — whatever channels your customers use. It remembers everything. It works 24/7. It gets smarter every week.

Under the hood: a multi-agent architecture with 100+ specialist capabilities. The customer never sees the complexity. They see one operator that handles everything.

**And when they're ready:** the operator can run on their own hardware, their own cloud, their own models. Data sovereignty is a module, not a separate product. BYOK is already built in. Private inference is a plug-in away.

## The Lead Generation Engine

Two parallel paths, both on the landing page:

1. **Embedded AI Diagnostic (Samantha)** — A live agent runs a 7-minute audit. Asks 5 questions. Delivers a personalized highest-leverage workflow recommendation. The lead magnet is created on the fly, unique to each visitor. This IS the demo.

2. **"Book a Live Demo" CTA** — Direct link to Remington's calendar. For serious buyers who want to skip the funnel and talk to a human.

The diagnostic is not the close. It's the proof of capability. The close happens on a founder-led call after the prospect has experienced the product firsthand.

See [02_LEAD_GENERATION_ENGINE.md](./02_LEAD_GENERATION_ENGINE.md) for the full funnel mechanics.

## The Sales Motion

| Principle | Execution |
|---|---|
| **Rule of 100** | 100 outbound touches daily. Non-negotiable. |
| **Demo-first** | The live demo is the strongest conversion tool. Show something amazing. |
| **Funnel sequence** | Amazing demo → beta code / 7-day trial → founder-led close call |
| **Anchor high** | Present Enterprise Sovereign (€250K+) and Sovereign (€195K) first. Even if they buy Foundation, they know what's possible. |
| **Cash margin** | Near-zero COGS on consulting and foundation tiers. Sovereign COGS ~€36K at 81.5% margin. Hardware is client-funded. |
| **Plant the module seed** | In every close call: "When you need data sovereignty, we can add private inference at any point — it's modular." |

See [03_SALES_MOTION.md](./03_SALES_MOTION.md) for the full close sequence, objection handling, and weekly operating rhythm.
