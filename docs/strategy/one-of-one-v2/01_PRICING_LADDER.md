## The Ladder

Five tiers + modular add-ons. One philosophy: **the free tier proves we're the best, the paid tiers capture the value, and modules expand the relationship over time.**

---

### Tier 0: Diagnostic (€0)

| Attribute | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Price** | Free |
| **What it is** | 7-minute AI-powered business audit on the landing page |
| **What the customer gets** | A personalized highest-leverage workflow recommendation, generated live by Samantha |
| **What we get** | A qualified lead who has experienced the product firsthand |
| **COGS** | ~€0.15–0.30 per session (LLM inference) |
| **Purpose** | Lead generation. Prove capability. Create desire. |

The diagnostic is not a demo in the traditional sense. The AI asks 5 questions about their business, analyzes their answers in real-time, and delivers a specific, actionable recommendation. The lead magnet is created on the fly — unique to each visitor. No PDF. No ebook. No generic content.

**Why this works:** The prospect experiences the product before any sales conversation happens. By the time they talk to Remington, they already believe.

### Tier 1: Consulting Sprint (€3,500)

| Attribute | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Price** | €3,500 one-time |
| **What it is** | Scope-only engagement. Deep diagnostic + implementation roadmap. |
| **Duration** | 1–2 weeks |
| **Deliverable** | Written scope document: what to automate, what to build, priority sequence, estimated ROI |
| **COGS** | ~€0 (founder time only) |
| **Gross margin** | ~100% |
| **Purpose** | First paid touchpoint. Builds trust. De-risks the Foundation purchase. |

The consulting sprint is the bridge between "I'm interested" and "I'm buying." It gives the customer a tangible deliverable they can use even if they don't proceed with implementation. It gives us a paying relationship and deep discovery.

**When to use:** For prospects who need to justify the spend internally, or who want to see the plan before committing to the build. Also works as a standalone offering for businesses that want strategy without implementation.

**Upsell path:** 80%+ of consulting sprint clients should convert to Foundation.

### Tier 4: Sovereign (€195,000)

| Attribute | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Setup price** | €195,000 |
| **Monthly** | €2,400/mo (after Year 1 — Year 1 support is included) |
| **What it is** | The full stack. Dream Team operator + private 4-node Mac Studio cluster + on-site installation + Year 1 managed support + monthly strategy calls. Everything, handled. |
| **Duration** | 4–6 weeks delivery |
| **What the customer gets** | Everything in Dream Team + 4x Mac Studio M3 Ultra (1TB unified memory), RDMA clustering, on-prem inference, on-site installation, 2-day team training, Year 1 Priority support, monthly strategy calls |
| **Hardware included** | 4x Mac Studio M3 Ultra 256GB, OWC Thunderbolt 5 hubs, 10GbE switch, rack, UPS |
| **Total COGS** | ~€36,000 (€28,804 hardware/infra + €1,000 operator + €4,200 Year 1 support + €2,000 travel) |
| **Gross margin** | ~81.5% |

Sovereign is the everything bundle. The client writes one check and gets a fully operational private AI platform — their own operator running on their own hardware, installed at their office, supported for a year. No vendor coordination, no integration risk, no project management.

**Payment terms:** 50% upfront (€97,500). Hardware ordered from client deposit. 50% on delivery.

**Cash flow:** Client deposit funds all hardware (~€29K) with ~€68K remaining before the build starts. You never spend your own money.

**The component math:** If a client bought these as separate modules, they'd spend €77K–€106K and coordinate it themselves. At €195K, the premium is for the white-glove experience: one contract, one vendor, everything handled.

## The Anchoring Psychology

The pricing page displays all tiers plus "Contact us" for Enterprise:

```
€0 Diagnostic  →  €3,500 Sprint  →  €7,000–€34,999 Foundation  →  €35,000+ Dream Team  →  €195,000 Sovereign  →  Enterprise: Contact Us
```

The anchoring works on two levels now:

1. **€195,000 Sovereign makes €7,000–€34,999 Foundation feel trivial.** "It's 3.5–18% of the full experience."
2. **Enterprise at €250K+ makes Sovereign feel reasonable.** "€195K gets me 80% of what a €300K deployment would."
3. **Sovereign is defensible.** 4 Mac Studios, Dream Team operator, on-site, Year 1 support — a sophisticated buyer can itemize it and see real value.
4. **The modules create a visible expansion path.** Foundation clients see that they can add private inference whenever they're ready. It's not a cliff — it's a ladder.

The SaaS tiers (€499/mo and €999/mo) are positioned as the ongoing relationship — "your AI's health insurance." They're not the buying decision. The setup fee is the buying decision. Modules expand the relationship when the client is ready.

## What's NOT on the Pricing Page

- No €49/month self-serve tier. That's the Year 2 "Tesla Model 3" play, funded by high-ticket profits.
- No free trial of the full product. The diagnostic IS the free experience. The product is paid.
- No annual discount. We don't need to incentivize annual commitments when the setup fee is the primary revenue.
- No separate "Infrastructure" page. Modules are add-ons to the existing tiers, mentioned as a capability on the pricing page, not a separate product.
