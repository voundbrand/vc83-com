80-90% of wealthy buyers have a "day one list" of trusted vendors. They buy from that list. If you're not on it, you don't exist.

Getting on the list requires social proof. Social proof requires clients. Clients require being on the list.

**The chicken-and-egg breaker: The Meta Glasses Live Demo + The Landing Page Diagnostic.**

Two weapons nobody else has:
1. A live demo where AI agents connect through Meta glasses and do real work in real-time — a spectacle people photograph and share
2. A landing page where an AI agent runs a personalized diagnostic in 7 minutes — proof of capability at scale, 24/7

### Talk Titles (Tested Framings)

| Version | Title | Audience |

Each talk is the same 45-minute structure:
- 5 min: The problem (AI hype vs. AI reality)
- 10 min: The framework (what actually works for €1M-20M businesses)
- 20 min: **THE LIVE META GLASSES DEMO** (the main event)
- 5 min: "One operator. Yours alone." — the product pitch
- 5 min: CTA -> sevenlayers.io diagnostic + "Book a Demo" + cards distributed

### Meta Glasses Demo Scenarios (Pick 2-3 per event)

| Scenario | What the Audience Sees | "Holy Shit" Moment |
|---|---|---|
| **The Live Lead Capture** | You walk through the room. Glasses identify a person. The operator drafts a personalized follow-up email in real-time based on the conversation it just heard. | The email references things said 30 seconds ago. |
| **The Live Client Q&A** | Someone asks a complex question. The operator pulls context from a knowledge base and answers through the glasses. | The agent knows the answer faster than any human could. |
| **The Operations Takeover** | Show a simulated incoming inquiry (WhatsApp message). The operator triages, classifies, drafts, schedules — all visible through the glasses. | 4 steps that take 15 minutes happen in 8 seconds. |
| **The Birthing (Mini)** | In 5 minutes, birth a new operator for a volunteer's business. Their phone buzzes with a WhatsApp message from their new AI. | Their AI knows their business and speaks to them directly. |

### Demo Rules

1. **Always live, never recorded.** The whole point is "this is happening right now."
2. **Always involve the audience.** Don't demo AT them. Demo WITH them.
3. **Always end with:** "That took [X] minutes. Imagine what it does in [X] days."
4. **Film everything.** Every live demo becomes LinkedIn content.

## Phase 1: Warm Network (Week 1-4)

**Leverage: HIGHEST. Cost: ZERO. Start TODAY.**

### Activity 1: The "Who Runs Events?" Blitz (Week 1)

Contact every person in your warm network and ask ONE question:

> "Hey [Name] — I just built something wild. AI agents that work through smart glasses in real-time. I'm looking for places to demo this live for business owners. Do you know anyone who organizes business events, runs a network, or leads a chapter of anything?"

| Contact Source | Volume | Expected Intros |
|---|---|---|
| WhatsApp contacts (business-related) | 50-100 messages | 5-10 intros to event organizers |
| LinkedIn connections | 100-200 messages | 10-20 intros |
| Former colleagues | 20-30 messages | 3-5 intros |
| Friends/family who know business owners | 30-50 messages | 2-5 intros |

**Expected outcome:** 20-40 introductions to people who organize events or run chapters.

### Activity 2: The "Living Room Demo" (Week 2-4)

5-10 private demos for warm network contacts. Not sales — market research.

> "I'm refining this demo before I take it to events. Can I show you in 15 minutes and get your honest reaction? Coffee's on me."

| Purpose | How It Helps |
|---|---|
| Refine the demo flow | Which scenarios get the biggest reactions? |
| Collect testimonial clips | Film reactions (with permission) |
| Generate referrals | Every person who sees it tells 3-5 others |
| Build confidence | By demo #10, you're bulletproof |

### Activity 3: Send to Landing Page (Ongoing from Week 2)

Every person you demo to or talk to: "Go to sevenlayers.io — talk to my AI. Tell me what you think."

The landing page diagnostic does the qualifying. You just need to get people there.

## Phase 2: Local Low-Barrier Events (Week 3-8)

**Leverage: HIGH. Cost: LOW. Fastest path to stage time.**

### Target 1: BNI Chapters in MV + Surrounding Region

**Why first:** Lowest barrier. Weekly meetings. Attend as guest, request education slot. No formal application.

**How to get in:**
1. Find chapters: bni.com/country/germany -> search MV, Rostock, Neubrandenburg, Schwerin, Berlin, Hamburg
2. Call: "Ich möchte als Gast teilnehmen. Ich habe eine KI-Demonstration mit Smart Glasses."
3. Attend -> do the 5-min glasses demo during education slot
4. Networking break: hand out QR cards -> sevenlayers.io

**Volume:** 2-4 BNI chapters in 6 weeks. Each chapter = 25-40 members.

**Expected outcome:** 3-5 warm leads per chapter visit. Invitations to come back. Referrals to OTHER chapters.

### Target 2: Wirtschaftsjunioren (Local Chapter)

Young business owners (under 40), tech-friendly, open to speakers.

Search "Wirtschaftsjunioren [nearest city]" -> email: "Ich biete einen 30-minütigen Impulsvortrag mit Live-Demo: KI-Agenten mit Smart Glasses für den Mittelstand."

**Lead time:** 4-8 weeks. **Expected:** 20-50 attendees, 5-10 warm leads.

### Target 3: Local Unternehmerstammtisch

Informal business owner meetups. Very low barrier.

- Search "Unternehmerstammtisch [your area]"
- Ask IHK: "Gibt es lokale Unternehmerrunden, bei denen ich einen Kurzvortrag halten könnte?"
- Check Meetup.com for "Unternehmer" groups in MV

**Lead time:** 1-4 weeks.
