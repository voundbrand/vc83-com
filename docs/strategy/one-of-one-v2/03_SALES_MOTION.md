## The Non-Negotiable: Rule of 100

100 outbound touches per day. Every day. No exceptions.

This is the Alex Hormozi discipline that makes everything else work. The diagnostic, the landing page, the BNI network — they're all multipliers on a base of consistent daily action.

| Touch Type | Daily Target | Weekly Total |
|---|---|---|
| LinkedIn DMs (value-first, not pitch-first) | 20 | 100 |
| WhatsApp/Signal messages to warm network | 10 | 50 |
| Email outreach (personalized, not template) | 10 | 50 |
| BNI 1:1 coffees / in-person conversations | 2 | 10 |
| Event follow-ups, referral requests | 5 | 25 |
| Comments/engagement on target prospects' posts | 15 | 75 |
| Landing page link shares (forums, groups, replies) | 10 | 50 |
| Podcast/speaking pitch emails | 3 | 15 |
| **Total daily touches** | **75–100** | **375–500/week** |

Not all touches are DMs. Comments, shares, and engagement count. The point is: every day, 100 people are reminded that Seven Layers exists.

## The Demo-First Philosophy

The live demo is the strongest conversion tool we have. The format matters less than showing something amazing.

| Demo Format | Context | Conversion Power |
|---|---|---|
| **Landing page diagnostic** | Online, self-serve, 24/7 | Medium (automated, no human warmth) |
| **Meta Glasses live demo** | BNI, events, in-person | Very high (theatrical, unforgettable) |
| **Founder-led video call** | Zoom/Meet after booking | High (personal, tailored) |
| **Phone demo** | Quick, after a warm intro | Medium-high (fast, low friction) |
| **In-app trial** | After diagnostic, 7-day access | High (extended experience, builds attachment) |

Every sales conversation should either start with or lead to a demo. Never pitch without showing. Never explain what you can demonstrate.

## The Close Call (30 Minutes)

### Structure

| Time | What Happens |
|---|---|
| 0:00–5:00 | **Recap the trial.** "What did you think? What surprised you?" Let them talk. |
| 5:00–10:00 | **Surface the pain.** "What's still costing you the most time/money?" Connect their trial experience to their real problems. |
| 10:00–15:00 | **Present the tiers.** Start with Sovereign (€195K). Then Dream Team. Then Foundation. Always anchor high. |
| 15:00–20:00 | **Recommend Foundation.** "Based on what you told me, Foundation is the right starting point. Here's what that looks like for your business specifically." |
| 20:00–25:00 | **Handle objections.** (See below.) |
| 25:00–30:00 | **Close or schedule next step.** "Ready to move forward?" or "What do you need to make a decision?" |

### The Anchor Script

> "Let me show you the full picture. For enterprise organizations with 500+ employees, we build custom private AI platforms — that's 10 Mac Studios, department-level routing, compliance logging — starting at €250,000."
>
> "Our Sovereign tier at €195,000 is the sweet spot for established businesses — you get a 4-node private compute cluster with a terabyte of memory, your own AI operator with custom specialists, on-site installation, and a full year of managed support. Your data never leaves your building."
>
> "For most businesses at your stage, Foundation is the right starting point. Starting at €7,000 excl. VAT to set up your operator, €499 a month to keep it running and learning. That's less than a part-time hire and it works 24/7."
>
> "And here's what I love about how we built this — when you need data sovereignty down the road, we just add a module. Your operator stays the same; we plug in private infrastructure underneath. It's not a separate project."
>
> "What makes more sense for where you are right now?"

They'll say Foundation. That's the plan. But you've planted two seeds: the Sovereign aspiration and the module expansion path.

## Objection Handling

| Objection | Response |
|---|---|
| **"€7,000 (excl. VAT) is a lot"** | "How much does your current bottleneck cost you per month? If your operator captures just 2–3 extra leads a month at your average deal size, it pays for itself in the first quarter. And it never takes a sick day." |
| **"Can I just use ChatGPT?"** | "ChatGPT forgets you every session. Your operator has 7 layers of memory that make it permanently yours. You talked to it for 7 days — it knows your business, your customers, your voice. ChatGPT starts from zero every time." |
| **"I need to think about it"** | "Completely understand. Your operator has learned a lot about your business this week. I can extend your trial another 7 days if you want more time. But everything it learned so far — that stays only if you continue." |
| **"My IT person can build this"** | "They probably could — in 6–12 months, for €100K+ in salary and infrastructure. I'll have it running in 2 weeks. And it'll be learning from every conversation from day one." |
| **"We're not tech-savvy"** | "That's exactly who this is for. You had a conversation with Samantha on the landing page — that's the entire user experience. If you can talk, you can use it." |
| **"What if it doesn't work?"** | "You already saw it work. You've been using it for 7 days. The question isn't if it works — it's how much more it can do with full implementation behind it." |
| **"Can I start with the consulting sprint?"** | "Absolutely. €3,500 excl. VAT gets you a full scope document — what to automate, what to build, priority sequence, estimated ROI. Most clients use that as their Foundation blueprint." |
| **"We can't send data to the cloud"** | "We hear that a lot — especially from law firms and clinics. Your operator can run on your own hardware. We set up a private compute cluster at your office. Same operator, same channels, but the data never leaves your building. We can add that to any tier as a module." |
| **"Can we use our own AI model?"** | "Absolutely. We support bring-your-own-key today — you can connect your own OpenAI or Anthropic account right now. And when you're ready, we can deploy open-source models on dedicated hardware or cloud GPUs, completely private to you. Your operator works with any inference backend." |
| **"What about GDPR / data sovereignty?"** | "Three options: BYOK so you control the API relationship, a dedicated GPU in an EU data center so nothing crosses borders, or hardware at your office for absolute sovereignty. All three work with your existing operator — it's modular." |
| **"€195K for Sovereign is a lot"** | "It includes everything: 4 Mac Studios with a terabyte of memory, your custom Dream Team operator, on-site installation, and a full year of managed support. If you bought those as modules separately, you'd spend €80-100K and coordinate it yourself. At €195K, we handle the entire project end-to-end." |

## Module Upsell Triggers (Existing Clients)

After a Foundation or Dream Team client has been active for 2+ months, listen for these signals in monthly check-ins or support conversations:

| Trigger | Module to Propose | Script |
|---|---|---|
| API costs mentioned / questioned | On-Prem Starter or Cloud Starter | "Your operator is running about €X/mo in inference right now. For €11,500 one-time, we can set up a Mac Studio at your office — you'd own the hardware and your per-month cost drops to €600. Pays for itself in [X] months." |
| "Can the AI read our contracts/docs?" | RAG Pipeline | "We can connect your operator to your internal documents — contracts, policies, whatever you need. It'll answer questions based on your actual content, not general knowledge. That's our RAG module — about €10K to set up." |
| Growing team size / more users | Tier upgrade + infrastructure module | "You've got [X] people using the operator now. Let's talk about dedicated infrastructure so everyone gets fast response times. We have a 4-node cluster that handles 50 concurrent users." |
| Compliance / audit mentioned | Compliance Package + On-Prem | "If you're preparing for [SOC 2 / HIPAA / audit], we have a compliance module — audit logging, PII redaction, retention policies. Pairs perfectly with on-prem hardware for full data sovereignty." |
| "The AI doesn't get our terminology" | Fine-Tuning | "That's a fine-tuning opportunity. We can train a custom model on your domain terminology so the operator speaks your language natively. About €15K and 4-6 weeks." |

**Rule:** Never hard-sell modules. These are expansion conversations that happen naturally when the client's needs evolve. The trigger phrases come from them, not from you.

## Payment Collection

| Tier | Payment Structure |
|---|---|
| Consulting Sprint | 100% upfront before work begins |
| Foundation | 100% setup upfront. Monthly billing starts after delivery. |
| Dream Team | 50% upfront, 50% on delivery. Monthly starts after delivery. |
| Sovereign | 50% upfront (€97,500). Hardware ordered from deposit. 50% on delivery. |

**Payment methods:** Bank transfer (primary for DACH), Stripe (cards), invoice with NET 7 for established businesses.

**Cash flow principle:** Never spend your own money on client work. The client's payment funds the build. Sovereign hardware is ordered after the deposit clears. Foundation delivery starts after payment. Cash-is-king means the cash arrives before the work begins.
