## English Description (30-Minute Demo)

Book a personal, founder-led 30-minute demo with Remington from Seven Layers.

In this session, we will review your current business bottlenecks, identify the highest-leverage workflow to automate first, and map how your private AI operator can run across your channels (web, voice, and messaging). You will leave with a clear next step: continue with the free diagnostic path, start a consulting sprint, or move into implementation.

Please bring one concrete operational pain point so we can make the call specific and actionable.
