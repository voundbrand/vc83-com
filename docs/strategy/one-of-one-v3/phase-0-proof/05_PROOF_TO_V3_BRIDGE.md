## What 5 SMB Customers Produce for v3

Every Phase 0 customer is an investment in the mid-market strategy. Here's exactly what 5 paying SMB customers unlock:

### 1. Case Studies with Real Data
Before/after metrics that mid-market buyers need to see before writing a €35K check:
- Missed calls reduced from X% to Y%
- Average response time dropped from Z minutes to under 10 seconds
- Appointments booked per week increased by N%
- Lead-to-appointment conversion improved by M%
- Owner hours reclaimed per week

These aren't projections. They're measurements from real businesses. Nothing else produces this.

### 2. Battle-Tested Demo
5 deployments = 5 rounds of tuning. Each customer surfaces edge cases, dialect variations, industry-specific language, and operational patterns that make the platform better. The demo a mid-market prospect sees in Phase 1 will be dramatically better than the one the first SMB customer saw in Phase 0 — because 5 real businesses have stress-tested it.

### 3. Objection Immunity
The single most damaging objection in enterprise sales: "Have you done this before?"

After Phase 0: "Yes. Five businesses are running on this right now. Here are their names, their results, and their phone numbers if you want to call them."

This is not a nice-to-have. This is table stakes for any deal above €10K.

### 4. Pricing Confidence
Phase 0 reveals what the market actually pays for, not what a strategy document hypothesizes. After 5 customers:
- Which agents deliver the most perceived value?
- What's the willingness-to-pay range?
- What does the customer talk about when they describe the product to someone else?
- Which features do they use daily vs. which do they forget exist?

This intelligence directly informs v3 tier structure and pricing.

### 5. Vertical Signal
Strategy documents hypothesize verticals. Customer data reveals them. After 5 customers:
- If 3 are in the same industry, you have a vertical to own.
- If they're spread across 5 industries, you have horizontal proof.
- Either way, you know where to point Phase 1 resources.

The vertical strategy crystallizes from deployment data, not market research.

### 6. Revenue
€10-15K MRR funds continued operation while selling upmarket. It's not venture-scale money — it's enough to keep building, keep selling, and not need to raise capital from a position of desperation. Phase 0 revenue buys time. Time buys options.

## Phase Gates

### Phase 0 → Phase 1 Gate

All criteria must be met before Phase 1 execution begins:

- [ ] **3+ paying customers retained 2+ months.** Not just signed — retained. They renewed because the product delivers value, not because they forgot to cancel.
- [ ] **2+ case studies with permission to use name/logo/data.** Written, formatted, and ready to send to prospects. At least one with the customer's real name and business.
- [ ] **Demo agent handles 90%+ of calls without intervention.** The platform runs without Remington babysitting every interaction. Edge cases are handled gracefully, not catastrophically.
- [ ] **One clear vertical pattern emerged.** The data from 5 deployments shows a cluster — whether that's dental, trades, professional services, or something unexpected.
- [ ] **€10K+ MRR.** Enough recurring revenue to fund solo operations for the next phase.

### Phase 1 → Phase 2 Gate

- [ ] **10+ paying customers.** Scale beyond what one person can manually manage (which forces building process and possibly hiring).
- [ ] **Inbound inquiries happening.** People finding sevenlayers without Remington reaching out first. Case studies, word of mouth, or content is pulling leads in.
- [ ] **One vertical dominates with repeatable motion.** Not just "we have customers in dental" but "we know the dental pitch, the dental pain points, the dental case study, and the dental close rate."
- [ ] **€30K+ MRR.** Revenue that funds a small team or meaningful go-to-market investment.
- [ ] **Can articulate what capital would accelerate.** If fundraising is an option, there's a specific, defensible answer to "what would you do with €1M?" — not "grow faster" but "hire 2 AEs for DACH dental vertical and achieve €100K MRR in 6 months."
