## Core Principle

**The product sells itself. My job is to get it in front of people and let them experience it.**

No pitch deck closes a deal. The demo call does. Every conversation is a path to "just call this number."

---

## The Demo Call Opener

This is the full 7-agent platform pitch. Memorize it. Deliver it with conviction.

> "What I'm about to show you is not a phone bot. It's seven AI specialists that run your front office. One answers every call. One coordinates scheduling. One qualifies leads. One handles documentation. One follows up with customers. One manages team operations. And one gives you real-time visibility. Instead of explaining it — just call this number."

Then shut up. Let them call. Let them experience it. The product does the rest.

## 90-Day Plan

### Week 1-2: Ship the Demo

- One universal demo agent live ("Schmitt & Partner" — see `04_PROOF_PHASE_DEMO.md`).
- All 7 agents visible and functional in the demo flow.
- Test the demo 50+ times. Call as different personas. Break it. Fix it. Polish it.
- Demo number live 24/7. If someone calls at 2am, Clara answers.
- Prepare the 1-pager PDF: "Call this number. Ask anything."

**Exit criteria:** Demo is rock-solid. You'd bet money on it handling any reasonable call.

### Week 3-6: Get in Rooms

- 10-15 warm conversations minimum. Sources:
  - Personal network (friends, family, former colleagues who run businesses)
  - BNI or similar networking groups
  - LinkedIn DMs to local business owners (Berlin and DACH region)
  - Local business events, meetups, chamber of commerce
- Every conversation ends with one of two outcomes:
  1. "I'll configure an AI team for your business — you and your team test it for 2 weeks, free." (if they have the pain)
  2. "Who do you know that's frustrated about missed calls?" (if they don't)
- No cold email blasts. No paid ads. One human talking to another human.

**Exit criteria:** 5-8 demo calls booked from warm conversations.

### Week 5-10: Run Trials

- 2-3 trials running simultaneously. More than 3 is unsustainable as a solo founder.
- v3 trial playbook applies (see `04_TRIAL_PLAYBOOK.md`):
  - Week 1: Guided testing with owner + key employees. Founder monitors and tunes. Daily summary.
  - Week 2: Widen the circle — friends, family, more employees stress-test the agent. Founder checks in every 2 days.
- No real customers call during the trial. The agent runs on a dedicated test number.
- Direct WhatsApp support with Remington for every trial customer.
- Document everything: scenarios tested, accuracy, edge cases, team feedback.

**Exit criteria:** 3-4 trials completed with clear go/no-go signal from each.

### Week 8-13: Convert and Collect Proof

- Close trials at Phase 0 pricing (€2-3K/month, no setup, month-to-month).
- Use the Phase 0 close script (see below).
- Collect proof artifacts from every customer:
  - Before/after data (missed calls, response times, appointments)
  - Written or video testimonial
  - Permission to use business name and logo
  - One-paragraph case study
- Begin using proof from Customer 1 to close Customer 3, 4, 5.

**Exit criteria:** 5 paying customers. €10-15K MRR. 2+ case studies ready.

## Solo Founder Weekly Rhythm

This is the constraint that makes Phase 0 work. Guard it.

| Day | Activity | Hours |
|---|---|---|
| **Monday** | 2 outreach conversations + trial monitoring | 3-4h |
| **Tuesday** | Product work (build, fix, improve) + trial check-ins | 5-6h |
| **Wednesday** | 1-2 demo calls + trial monitoring | 3-4h |
| **Thursday** | Product work (build, fix, improve) | 4-5h |
| **Friday** | 1 LinkedIn post + admin + weekly review | 2-3h |

**Totals:**
- ~10-12 hours/week selling (outreach, demos, trial support, closing)
- ~15-20 hours/week building (product, infrastructure, demo polish)

These overlap. A trial check-in call that surfaces a bug is both selling and building. Don't over-optimize the split — just make sure neither gets to zero in any given week.

## Pipeline Math (Honest)

```
15 warm conversations
  → 5-8 demo calls (people who actually see the demo)
    → 3-4 trials (people who try it on their business)
      → 2-3 customers (people who pay)
```

Repeat this cycle twice in 90 days = 5 customers.

**This is NOT a funnel — it's a hustle.** There's no top-of-funnel content, no nurture sequence, no marketing automation. It's one founder having conversations and letting the product speak.

Conversion assumptions:
- Warm conversation → demo call: ~40-50% (pain is present and they're curious)
- Demo call → trial: ~50-60% (the demo is genuinely impressive)
- Trial → customer: ~60-70% (they've seen it work on their business)

These are aggressive but realistic for founder-led sales with a product that demos well.

## 4-Phase Sales Structure

The v3 sales structure (from `02_SALES_MOTION.md`) applies at Phase 0 scale:

### Phase 1: Demo
- Deliver the opener. Let them call the demo number.
- If on a call: screen share and walk through together.
- If in person: hand them your phone or WhatsApp the number.
- Duration: 10-15 minutes. The demo does the talking.

### Phase 2: Demo Kit
- Send the 1-pager PDF after the demo call.
- Include the demo number so they can call again (they will).
- "What to Watch For" checklist from `03_DEMO_KIT_SPEC.md` — adapted for Phase 0 (see `04_PROOF_PHASE_DEMO.md`).

### Phase 3: Trial (Internal Testing)
- 2-week free test on a dedicated number. No real customers involved.
- Founder configures their agent team.
- Owner, employees, friends, and family test the agent.
- Daily summaries in Week 1, every-other-day in Week 2.
- WhatsApp support line direct to Remington.

### Phase 4: Close
- Trial ends. Review the data together.
- Use the Phase 0 close script (below).
- Sign month-to-month at Phase 0 pricing.

## Phase 0 Close Script

Anchor at v3 pricing. Offer Phase 0 pricing as exclusive early-adopter positioning.

> "Your team has tested this for two weeks. They've called it, tried to break it, and seen how it handles your business. Companies will pay €35K or more to implement this — and they will, once we have the case studies to prove it. Right now, I'm building those case studies. You're one of our first five customers, which means you get founder-level attention at early-adopter pricing: €2-3K per month, no setup fee, cancel anytime. The next step is simple: we put this on your real line and let it do the same thing for your actual customers. The only thing I ask in return is permission to share your results — anonymized or named, your choice."

Key elements:
- Anchor high (€35K) so €2-3K feels like a steal.
- Frame them as early adopters, not discount customers.
- The "permission to share results" ask creates reciprocity and secures the case study.
- Month-to-month removes risk.

## Objection Handling

All objection handling from the v3 `02_SALES_MOTION.md` remains valid. Phase 0 adds these:

**"You don't have any other customers."**
> "That's exactly why you're getting this pricing. In 6 months, when we have 15 customers and case studies from every industry, this will cost 5x more. You're getting in early."

**"What if you shut down?"**
> "Month-to-month. If we disappear, you stop paying. You've risked nothing except 2 weeks of internal testing — and your team already knows it works."

**"I need to think about it."**
> "Totally fair. While you think, the demo number is live 24/7. Call it again whenever you want. I'll check in on Thursday."

**"Can you do it cheaper?"**
> "€2K/month is already below what we'll charge in Phase 1. At this price, I'm essentially investing my time in your business to build a case study. I can't go lower and sustain the level of support you'll get."
