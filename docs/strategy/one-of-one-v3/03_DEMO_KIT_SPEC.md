## What the Demo Kit Is

A package the business owner receives after their demo call so they can demonstrate the phone agent to their own team or board **without Remington present**. The goal: the owner becomes the internal champion. The Demo Kit makes them look like a hero.

The Demo Kit exists because buying decisions at mid-market companies are never solo. The owner needs to sell internally. We give them the ammunition to do that without needing us in the room.

---

## Components

The Demo Kit contains six items. Each one serves a specific purpose in the internal sell.

### Component 1: Configured Demo Phone Agent

| Attribute | Specification |
|-----------|---------------|
| **What it is** | A live phone number connected to an agent configured for a fictional business in the owner's industry |
| **Availability** | 24/7 during the demo period |
| **Languages** | German (primary), English (if needed) |
| **Purpose** | Let anyone on the team call, at any time, and experience the product firsthand |

**Industry-Specific Demo Agents:**

| Vertical | Fictional Business | Agent Knowledge |
|----------|--------------------|-----------------|
| **Medical (MVZ)** | Praxis Dr. Weber | Appointment types, insurance questions, location hours, patient intake flows, multi-location routing |
| **Real Estate** | Immobilien Schmidt | Active listings, viewing bookings, buyer qualification questions, price ranges by neighbourhood, agent availability |
| **Mittelstand Services** | Muller Elektrotechnik | Service request intake, urgency dispatch triage, quote generation, emergency rates, coverage areas |

Each demo agent is pre-loaded with realistic business knowledge so it performs convincingly in every conversation. The owner's team should not be able to tell it apart from a well-trained receptionist.

---

### Component 2: Demo Phone Number Card

| Attribute | Specification |
|-----------|---------------|
| **In-person meeting** | Physical card, premium stock, clean design |
| **Remote meeting** | Digital card delivered via email and WhatsApp |
| **Design elements** | Phone number, "Call me" tagline, QR code that dials the number |
| **Purpose** | Make it trivially easy for anyone on the team to try the agent |

The card is designed to be passed around. Leave it on the conference table. Share it in the team group chat. The lower the friction to call, the more people who experience the agent firsthand.

---

### Component 3: "How to Demo This" 1-Pager

A PDF document the owner uses to run the internal demo themselves.

**Contents:**

| Section | Content |
|---------|---------|
| **Call to action** | "Call this number: [number]" |
| **Suggested questions** | 3 industry-specific questions (see below) |
| **Key observation** | "Notice: The agent knows the business. Books appointments. Follows up. 24/7." |
| **Internal pitch line** | "This is what it would do for us." |
| **60-second intro script** | Word-for-word script for the owner to introduce the demo |

**Industry-Specific Demo Questions:**

| Vertical | Question 1 | Question 2 | Question 3 |
|----------|-----------|-----------|-----------|
| **Medical** | "Do you have an appointment available this week?" | "Which location accepts my insurance?" | "I need to reschedule my appointment." |
| **Real Estate** | "I'm looking for a 3-bedroom apartment in Mitte." | "Can I book a viewing for Saturday?" | "What's the price range for properties in Charlottenburg?" |
| **Services** | "My heating broke down, can someone come today?" | "I need a quote for rewiring my office." | "What are your emergency rates?" |

**60-Second Intro Script (template):**

> "I want to show you something. Call this number. Ask it anything about [business type]. This is what our customers would experience when nobody picks up. It knows our services, it books appointments, it handles questions — and it's available 24/7. Just try it."

### Component 4: ROI Calculator

| Attribute | Specification |
|-----------|---------------|
| **Format** | Google Sheets, shared link with edit access |
| **Pre-filled** | Yes, with numbers captured during the demo call |
| **Editable** | Yes, owner can adjust any input and see impact immediately |
| **Purpose** | Turn gut feeling into financial justification for the team/board |

**Calculator Structure:**

| Row | Label | Example Value | Formula |
|-----|-------|---------------|---------|
| 1 | Current missed calls per week | 15 | Estimated during demo call |
| 2 | Percentage that are potential new customers | 40% | Estimated during demo call |
| 3 | Average customer lifetime value | EUR 2,000 | Estimated during demo call |
| 4 | Monthly revenue loss from missed calls | EUR 4,800 | = Row1 x 4 weeks x Row2 x Row3 |
| 5 | Number of locations | 8 | From demo call |
| 6 | Total monthly revenue loss across all locations | EUR 38,400 | = Row4 x Row5 |
| 7 | Our monthly cost | EUR 2,500 | Based on Multi-Location tier |
| 8 | Net monthly gain | EUR 35,900 | = Row6 - Row7 |
| 9 | Setup cost | EUR 35,000 | Based on Dream Team tier |
| 10 | Payback period in months | 0.97 months | = Row9 / Row8 |

The numbers are pre-filled but editable. The owner can adjust assumptions and see the payback period update in real-time. The math should be self-evidently compelling — if it is not, the prospect is not the right ICP.

### Component 5: Founder Support Channel

| Attribute | Specification |
|-----------|---------------|
| **Channel** | Dedicated Slack channel or WhatsApp group (owner's preference) |
| **Availability** | Remington available throughout the demo period |
| **Response time** | Same-day for questions, within the hour for urgent issues |
| **Tone** | Collaborative, not salesy |

**Communication Guidelines:**

| Do | Do Not |
|----|--------|
| "How did the team react?" | "Ready to sign?" |
| "What questions came up?" | "Should I send the contract?" |
| "Let me know if anyone has trouble calling" | "We have limited spots available" |
| "That's great feedback — we hear that a lot" | "The price goes up next month" |

The purpose of the channel is to make the owner feel supported. If the team is impressed, celebrate with them. If questions come up, answer immediately. The founder support channel turns Remington into a trusted advisor, not a vendor.

### Component 6: "What to Watch For" Checklist

A printed or digital one-pager the owner can use while their team tries the agent.

| # | Observation Item | Notes Column |
|---|-----------------|--------------|
| 1 | Did the agent answer within 3 rings? | |
| 2 | Did it know the business? | |
| 3 | Could it book an appointment? | |
| 4 | Did it handle an unusual question gracefully? | |
| 5 | Did it escalate when it should have? | |
| 6 | Would this work for us during off-hours? | |
| 7 | Would this work across all our locations? | |

The checklist reframes the experience from "cool demo" to "business evaluation." Each item maps to a real operational concern. When the owner fills it out with checkmarks, they have built their own internal business case.

## Delivery

| Step | Timing | Details |
|------|--------|---------|
| **Send digital assets** | Within 24 hours of demo call | Email with: PDF 1-pager, ROI calculator link, checklist, demo phone number, support channel invite |
| **Send physical card** | Same day if in-person meeting | Hand-delivered or mailed with the phone number card |
| **Demo period** | 3–7 days | Owner sets the pace; follow up on Day 2 and Day 5 |
| **Close the loop** | End of demo period | "How did the team react? Ready for a trial?" |

## Industry Variants

| Element | Medical (MVZ) | Real Estate | Mittelstand Services |
|---------|---------------|-------------|---------------------|
| **Demo agent** | Praxis Dr. Weber | Immobilien Schmidt | Muller Elektrotechnik |
| **Demo questions** | Appointments, insurance, rescheduling | Listings, viewings, pricing | Service requests, urgency, quotes |
| **ROI pre-fill** | Higher LTV (EUR 3K–5K), lower missed call % | Higher missed call %, lower LTV | Moderate across both dimensions |
| **1-pager tone** | Patient care and compliance | Buyer conversion and speed | Service reliability and coverage |
| **Checklist emphasis** | Escalation, insurance handling | Qualification accuracy, booking | Urgency dispatch, quote accuracy |

The ROI calculator template is the same across all verticals — only the pre-filled values differ based on what was discussed during the demo call.

## Success Criteria

The Demo Kit worked when the owner comes back and says:

> "My team tried it. They're impressed. Let's do a trial."

That sentence is the only metric that matters. Everything in the Demo Kit — the live agent, the card, the script, the calculator, the checklist — exists to produce that sentence.
