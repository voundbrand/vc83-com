> Operative — companion to v3 GTM strategy

---

## TL;DR

telli is a Berlin-based, YC-backed AI voice agent platform founded by ex-Enpal operators. They raised $3.6M pre-seed (Cherry Ventures + YC), hit $2M+ ARR within ~5 months of launch, and processed ~1M calls with a team of 16. They sell AI-powered outbound + inbound call automation to B2C mid-market companies (solar, real estate, HR, insurance). Their positioning is "human-AI hybrid" — AI handles predictable interactions, humans handle high-value moments.

**Why they matter to us:** Same geography (Berlin), same investor class (YC), overlapping target verticals (real estate, energy, multi-location services), and similar "AI operator" narrative. But fundamentally different architecture, go-to-market, and value proposition.

## 4. Product

### Core Offering
AI call automation platform for outbound and inbound phone operations. Positions as a **human-AI orchestration layer** rather than full replacement.

### Capabilities

**Outbound:**
- Lead qualification and conversion (+12% conversion improvement)
- Lead reactivation across buying journey (recovered $120K+ revenue)
- Smart calling strategy (optimal timing, dynamic number rotation)
- Branded caller ID with company logos
- Automatic callbacks

**Inbound:**
- 24/7 AI receptionist (+53% reachability)
- Customer service resolution (+21% first-resolution rate)
- Warm transfers to human agents (<10s)

**Appointments:**
- Multi-timezone scheduling (+33% booking rate)
- Direct calendar integration during live calls

**Multi-Channel:**
- Voice (primary), SMS, WhatsApp, Email

### Platform Features
- Custom voices via professional actors + AI cloning (Cartesia)
- Can clone a client's top sales rep's voice
- Real-time call monitoring and control
- Transcription, summarization, sentiment analysis
- A/B testing on call scripts
- CRM integration
- API (docs.telli.com)

### Tech Stack

| Layer | Technology |
|---|---|
| **Voice/TTS** | Cartesia AI (primary), ElevenLabs |
| **LLMs** | OpenAI + Anthropic Claude mix |
| **Architecture** | Stateful agents (remember past calls across customer journey) |
| **Compliance** | GDPR certified, EU AI Act compliant |

## 9. Head-to-Head: One of One vs. telli

### Where We Overlap

| Dimension | Overlap |
|---|---|
| **Geography** | Both Berlin-based, DACH-first |
| **Trust posture** | Both GDPR-first, EU-hosted, privacy-forward |
| **AI voice** | Both use ElevenLabs + custom voice tech |
| **Target** | Both pursue mid-market multi-location businesses |
| **Verticals** | Real estate, energy/HVAC, services |
| **Narrative** | Both say "AI operator" / "AI team" not "chatbot" |

### Where We Diverge — Critical Differences

| Dimension | One of One (sevenlayers) | telli |
|---|---|---|
| **Core product** | Multi-agent platform (7 specialist AIs across voice, scheduling, docs, ops, analytics) | Single-purpose call automation (outbound + inbound dialer) |
| **Scope** | Full business operating system — phone, WhatsApp, email, SMS, webchat, scheduling, documentation, team ops, location intelligence | Phone calls only (with SMS/WhatsApp/email as secondary channels) |
| **Architecture** | Multi-agent orchestration with trust telemetry, multi-device, templates | Stateful single-agent per call, smart dialer infrastructure |
| **Sales motion** | Founder-led demo → 2-week free trial at one location → roll out | Enterprise sales, custom pricing, inbound-driven |
| **Deal size** | €35K–€195K implementation + monthly | Custom enterprise (likely similar range given $2M ARR) |
| **Entry point** | Free live demo + free 2-week trial proving ROI at one location | No public trial model visible |
| **Primary value** | "One intelligent front door" — unified customer experience across all channels and locations | "AI handles the repetitive calls" — efficiency and volume |
| **Customer pain** | "Every missed call costs you a customer" — availability and revenue recovery | "Reps shouldn't waste time on qualifying questions" — cost and efficiency |
| **Team model** | Named AI specialists (Clara, Maren, Jonas, etc.) with distinct roles | Generic AI agent voices (cloned from top performers) |
| **Unique tech** | Trust telemetry, multi-location intelligence, cross-agent orchestration | Smart dialing, number rotation, connection rate optimization |

### Their Strengths (Honest Assessment)

1. **Traction is real** — $2M+ ARR in 5 months, 1M calls, 50%+ MoM growth
2. **YC + Cherry Ventures** gives them fundraising momentum and SF network
3. **Enpal as a reference customer** is powerful social proof in German energy/services
4. **Operator founders** — they lived the problem, and investors buy that narrative
5. **Simple pitch** — "AI makes your calls" is easier to explain than "AI operates your business"
6. **80%+ inbound acquisition** — product-market fit signal is strong

### Their Weaknesses (Our Opportunities)

1. **Single-channel dependency** — They are a phone company. We are an operating system. When voice becomes commoditized (it will), they need to expand or die.
2. **No trial model** — We offer 2-week free proof at one location. They require enterprise sales cycles.
3. **No multi-agent architecture** — They have one AI per call. We have 7 specialists working together across the entire business workflow.
4. **Outbound-heavy positioning** — Their pitch leads with "AI sales agent + auto dialer." Mid-market service businesses (our ICP) don't have outbound call centers — they have inbound chaos.
5. **No location intelligence** — They optimize individual calls. We optimize across locations with Nora's analytics.
6. **Generic "AI agent" branding** — We have named, specialized agents with distinct personalities. Their agents are fungible.
7. **No documentation/ops layer** — Tobias (field docs), Kai (team ops) have no equivalent in telli's product.
8. **B2C focus** — Their case studies are all B2C outbound (solar leads, moving quotes). Our ICP is B2B service businesses with inbound complexity.

## 10. Competitive Positioning Playbook

### When a prospect asks "How are you different from telli?"

> "telli is great at one thing: making outbound sales calls faster. If you need an AI auto-dialer to cold-call leads, they're solid. But that's not your problem, is it?
>
> Your problem is that calls are falling through the cracks across 8 locations. Your team can't coordinate schedules. You don't know which location is underperforming until it's too late. And when a customer calls at 7 PM, nobody picks up.
>
> We don't just answer phones — we run your front office. Seven AI specialists handle reception, scheduling, lead routing, documentation, follow-up, team coordination, and location analytics. telli gives you a faster dialer. We give you an intelligent business."

### When a prospect says "But telli has YC backing and big logos"

> "Good companies attract good investors. We respect that. But look at what those logos actually use telli for — Enpal uses them to dial leads faster. McMakler uses them to reduce wait times. That's call center optimization, not business transformation.
>
> Our customers don't just make more calls — they recover 34% of missed revenue, cut response times from hours to seconds, and have real-time visibility across every location. We prove that in 14 days with your real data, at your real location, for free."

### Positioning Framework

```
telli = AI DIALER (faster calls)
One of One = AI OPERATOR (smarter business)

They optimize the phone.
We optimize the business that answers the phone.
```

## 11. Strategic Implications for v3

### 1. Validate Our Differentiation Is Real
telli's success proves the market exists. But their traction is in outbound B2C — solar leads, moving quotes, HR screening. Our v3 pivot to mid-market multi-location service businesses (inbound-heavy, ops-complex) is a genuinely different ICP. **The risk of direct collision is low today but rises if they expand scope.**

### 2. Speed Matters
telli went from founding to $2M ARR in ~8 months. They will raise a Series A (likely $15-25M) in H2 2026. Once they have that capital, they will expand beyond calls into scheduling, follow-up, and ops — directly into our territory. **Our 12-18 month window to establish mid-market multi-location as "our" category is real.**

### 3. Trial-First Is Our Moat
telli has no public trial. They sell enterprise contracts. Our 2-week free trial at one location is a structural advantage — it lowers the barrier and creates data-driven proof that enterprise sales can't replicate.

### 4. Named Agents Are Defensible
telli's agents are interchangeable voice skins. Clara, Maren, Jonas, Tobias, Lina, Kai, Nora are named specialists with defined roles. This creates brand stickiness, easier onboarding ("just add Clara"), and a product narrative that's harder to copy.

### 5. Watch Their Hiring
telli is actively hiring ops, engineering, and design roles in Berlin. Their roadmap will become visible through job postings. Monitor for "scheduling," "multi-channel," "operations," or "analytics" roles — those signal scope expansion toward our territory.

### 6. Monitor Their Series A
When Cherry Ventures leads a Series A (likely Q3-Q4 2026), the press cycle will include their roadmap narrative. That's when we'll know if they're staying in calls or moving into operations.
