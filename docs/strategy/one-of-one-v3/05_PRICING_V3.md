## Pricing Philosophy

> "The trial is free. The product is not. The data from the trial makes the price irrelevant."

We lead with value, not price. The 2-week trial generates enough data to justify the investment. Pricing is presented AFTER the trial, when the business case is self-evident.

---

## Tier Overview

| Tier | Setup (excl. VAT) | Monthly (excl. VAT) | Who It's For |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| **2-Week Trial** | €0 | €0 | One location, one department. Prove the value with real data. |
| **Single Location** | €12,000 | €999/mo | Keep the agent running after trial. One phone line, one calendar, ongoing learning. |
| **Multi-Location (3--10 sites)** | €35,000+ | €2,500/mo | Dream Team deployment with location-aware routing, unified reporting, cross-location scheduling. |
| **Enterprise (10+ sites)** | €100,000--€195,000 | Custom (€5,000--€10,000/mo) | Full Sovereign deployment. Private infrastructure option. On-site installation. Year 1 managed support. |
| **Foundation (fallback)** | €7,000 | €499/mo | For smaller businesses that come through the funnel. v2 tier, still available. |

---

## Why Pricing Changed from v2

| Change | Rationale |
| --- | --- |
| Single Location (€12K) replaces low-end Foundation for trial conversions | Higher ACV reflects proven value from trial. Owner saw the data — €12K is justified. |
| Multi-Location (€35K+) is the primary target | Dream Team repackaged for multi-site deployment. This is where the platform shines. |
| Enterprise (€100K--€195K) | Sovereign tier repositioned as "10+ locations" rather than infrastructure-first. Includes on-prem option. |
| Foundation kept at €7K + €499/mo | Catch-all for leads too small for mid-market but still valuable. Not promoted, not hidden. |

## The Close Script (pricing section)

> "Let me show you the options.
>
> For organizations with 10+ locations, we deploy a full enterprise system — private infrastructure, dedicated support, the works. That starts at €100,000.
>
> For your scale — [N] locations — Multi-Location makes the most sense. €35,000 setup, €2,500 a month. That covers location-aware routing, unified reporting, and cross-location scheduling. Every location gets its own agent, but they share knowledge.
>
> If you want to start with just this one location from the trial, that's €12,000 setup, €999 a month. And when you're ready to expand, we scale it.
>
> Based on your trial data — [X] calls handled, [Y] appointments booked — you're looking at €[Z] per month in recovered revenue per location. The setup pays for itself in [M] months.
>
> What makes sense for where you are right now?"
