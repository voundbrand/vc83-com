# GDPR Compliance Cost Estimate

Estimated costs for a small-to-medium SaaS startup.

---

## One-Time Costs

| Item | Low Estimate | High Estimate | Notes |
|------|-------------|---------------|-------|
| GDPR lawyer review of all documents | 1,500 EUR | 5,000 EUR | Privacy policy, terms, cookie policy, DPA |
| Cookie consent implementation | 0 EUR | 500 EUR | Build yourself (0) or use Cookiebot/Osano (paid) |
| Engineering time for code changes | Internal | Internal | See `03_CODE_CHANGES.md` for scope |
| Initial penetration test | 3,000 EUR | 15,000 EUR | External firm, scope-dependent |
| Privacy impact assessment | 0 EUR | 3,000 EUR | DIY or hire consultant |
| **Total one-time** | **4,500 EUR** | **23,500 EUR** | |

## Cost of Non-Compliance

For context:

| Risk | Potential Cost |
|------|---------------|
| GDPR fine (minor violations) | Up to 10 million EUR or 2% annual revenue |
| GDPR fine (major violations) | Up to 20 million EUR or 4% annual revenue |
| Lost enterprise customer (no DPA available) | Varies - potentially significant |
| Data breach costs (notification, legal, remediation) | 10,000-500,000+ EUR |
| Reputation damage | Unquantifiable |

The investment in compliance is small compared to the risk of non-compliance.
