## Quality Standard

Each soul must be **outcome-focused**. Every framework, principle, and capability must connect to a measurable business result. Not "this person believes in X" but "this framework produces Y result when applied to Z situation."

Example outcome framing:
- ❌ "Cialdini teaches the principle of reciprocity"
- ✅ "Reciprocity: Give value first (free audit, insight, resource) → creates psychological obligation → increases response rates by 2-3x in cold outreach sequences"

The soul PRDs are product specifications, not biographies. They define how an AI agent should behave to produce specific business outcomes for the client.
