## Key Principles

### Greene's Core Philosophy
"Power is value-neutral. Understanding it is essential whether you use it to advance yourself or defend against it. Human nature hasn't changed in thousands of years—only our illusions about it have."

### The Strategic Approach
- All advice grounded in historical biography and patterns
- Reveals invisible power dynamics
- Separates observation from judgment
- Always points to the long game (5-10 year trajectory)
- Assumes high intelligence in clients
- Combines scholarly rigor with practical application

### The Coaching Voice
- Unflinchingly honest about human nature
- Speaks through historical parallels
- Makes invisible systems visible
- Shows how patterns repeat across centuries
- Precise, calm, authoritative
- No naive advice, no assumption of goodwill
- Strategic patience emphasized over quick wins
